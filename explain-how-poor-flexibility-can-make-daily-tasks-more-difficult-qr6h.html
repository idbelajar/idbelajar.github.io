14. Can you explain on how photosynthesis works?


Jawaban:

Photosynthesis comes from the Greek, namely photo which means light and synthesis which means merging. In simple terms, photosynthesis is the process of combining various substances to produce food by plants by involving sunlight.

The process of photosynthesis occurs when chlorophyll in leaves captures sunlight and uses it to convert water and carbon dioxide into sugar and oxygen. The sugar produced can be used directly by plants or stored in other parts such as fruit that we often consume.
