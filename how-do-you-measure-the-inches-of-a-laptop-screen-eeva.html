Make sure the pan settles properly.Close the lid frimly.Plug in and press the switch. The light indicating"COOK"will be on. The cooking will start immediately When the rice is cooked, the switch move up to the keep warm, and the light indicating "WARM" will be on. After the swicht moves up, leave the lid closed for at least 15 minutes to steam the rice fully. Scoop and mix the rice well. Unplug.
