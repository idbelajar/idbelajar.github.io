5. memperkenalkan diri dan kegiatan sehari hari dalam bahasa inggris


Answer:

Here’s an example of introduction and a daily routine.

Good morning/afternoon/evening my friends.

I want to introduce myself. My name is Natalie White. You can call me Nath. I come from New York. I was born in Los Angeles, on 15 April 1995. I moved from New York to this city to continue my study. I live in an apartment at White Summer street number 125. My hobbies are watching movies, reading novels, and swimming. From Monday to Friday, I usually wake up at 6 am because I have to go to the campus in the morning. After getting up, I make up my bed and then I take a bath. After that, I dress up and prepare my breakfast. I make my own breakfast because I live alone. I go to the campus at 7.30 am. The class usually finishes at 1 pm. I usually have a lunch at the campus before going home. After that I go home and then do my homework. I like to do my hobbies during my leisure time. In the evening, I cook my own dinner. After that, I prepare some books based on the schedule. I sleep at 11 pm. On weekend, I like to hang out with my friends.

That’s the end of my introduction, thank you and nice to meet you guys.

 

I hope the answer is complete enough.

7. Buatlah kegiatan sehari hari dalam bahasa inggris​


From Monday to Saturday, I usually wake up at 5 o’clock in the morning. I need to prepare myself to school. There are many activities I have to do before going to school.

First of all, I do my bed until it is very tidy. After that, I sweep the floor. I love to see my room neat and clean. When I still feel sleepy, I walk around my house. Also, I do some physical movements to stretch my body.

Then, I prepare some books based on schedule. I do not want to leave any book at home. I wash my face, brush my teeth, then have a shower. Finally, I eat breakfast after wearing my school uniform. Before going to school I hug my parents. I enjoy learning time at school. I like to play with my friends too.

The school finishes at 1 p.m. After school, I change my uniform then have lunch. I usually help parents to cook for our dinner then help to wash dishes after eating. After watching TV for one hour, I study and do my homework. At the end, I go to sleep at 9 p.m
