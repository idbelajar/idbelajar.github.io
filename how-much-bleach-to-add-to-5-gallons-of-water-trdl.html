14. To bring 1 kg of water from 25⁰C to 99 ⁰C takes how much heat input? (C for water = 4.18 J/g ⁰C)


Jawaban:

Heat to be input is 309 320 J

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

to be known :

m = 1 kg

t1 = 25°C

t2 = 99°C

C = 4.18 J/g°C = 4180 J/kg°C

to be asked :

Heat (Q) = . . . ?

solving :

Q = m × C × T

Q = 1 × 4180 × (99-25)

Q = 1 × 4180 × 74

Q = 309 320 J
