9. the length of the mercury thread in a liquid-in-glass thermometer is 3 cm at 0°C and 28 cm at 100°C. What is the length of mercury if the temperature is 40°C? in a way!!


ZAT dan KALOR

• Suhu

— skala termometer


0 °C ←→ 3 cm

100 °C ←→ 28 cm

40 °C ←→ ... cm


(x - 3) / (28 - 3) = (40 - 0) / (100 - 0)

(x - 3) / 25 = 40 / 100

x - 3 = 40 / 4

x = 13 cm ✔️


the length of mercury is 13 cm.


10. Mercury is a liquid metal. Its density is 13.6 g/cm' Mercury is supplied in a plastic bottle whose volume is 50 cm. The empty bottle has a mass of 25.09. Calculate the mass of the bottle when it is filled with mercury.​


Jawaban:

densitas = massa dibagi volume. Jadi, massa = densitas x volume

Massa merkuri: 13.6 x 50 = 680 g/cm2

Massa botol: 25.09

Total massa botol penuh merkuri: 680 + 25.09 = 705.09
