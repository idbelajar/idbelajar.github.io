1. A Rectangular Swimming Pool Is 25 Meters Long 6 Meters Wide. What Is The Area Of The Swimming Pool?


Jawaban:

150 m²

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Kolam renang persegi panjang dengan panjang 25 Meter, lebar 6 Meter. Berapa luas kolam renang tersebut ?

Panjang = 25 meter

Lebar = 6 meter

Ditanya = Luas

Luas = panjang dikali lebar

= p × l

= 25 m × 6 m

Luas = 150 m²

Maka, luas kolam renang persegi panjang tersebut adalah 150 m².

So, the area of the rectangular swimming pool is 150 m².

14. A private rectangular swimming pool has a length of 20 m and a breadth of 17 m. The swimming pool is surrounded by a walkway of breadth 1,5 m. The area of the walkway is.


﻿What's known:

﻿A rectangular swimming pool with,

﻿length = 20 m

﻿breadth = 17 m

﻿And it's surrounded by a walkway with,

﻿breadth = 1.5 m

﻿﻿The question:

The area of the walkway.

Answer:

The Area of the swimming pool without the walkway or A1 for short.

﻿length = 20 m

﻿breadth = 17 m

﻿Area = length × breadth

﻿A = 20 × 17

﻿A = 340 m² (A1)

﻿The Area of the swimming pool including the walkway or A2 for short. (+1.5 m)

﻿length = 21.5 m

breadth = 18.5 m

﻿Area = length × breadth

﻿A﻿ = 21.5 × 18.5

﻿A = 397.75 m² (A2)

﻿

﻿Area of the walkway,

﻿A2 - A1

397.75 - 340

﻿57.75 m²

﻿So the Area of the walkway is 57.75 m²
