15. . The area of a land is 16 m2 the area of this land in the map is 4 cm2 . a. write the scale of the map as simply as possible. b. The distance between two town is 5,4 km. How far apart are the towns on the map ?


Materi : Perbandingan dan Skala

Bagian A

Skala = Peta : Sebenarnya

Skala = 4 cm² : 16 m²

Skala = 4 cm² : ( 16 × 10.000 )cm²

Skala = 4 : 160.000

( Skala = 1 : 40.000 )

Bagian B

Peta = Sebenarnya × Skala

Peta = 5,4 km × 1 : 40.000

Peta = ( 5,4 × 100.000 ) cm × 1/40.000

Peta = 540.000 cm × 1/40.000

{ Peta = 13,5 cm }
