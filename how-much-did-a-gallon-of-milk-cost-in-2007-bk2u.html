Budget = Rp 600.000,00
Use to buy: 2 Cartons of milk, chocolate, cereal, a case of drawing charcoals.
Change = Rp 52.000,00
Budget used = Rp 600.000,00 - Rp 52.000,00 = Rp 548.000,00

a.
Charcoals = Budget used - 2 Cartons of milk - Chocolate - 1 Box of cereal
Charcoals = Rp 548.000,00 - Rp 25.000,00 - Rp 250.000,00 - Rp 74.000,00
Charcoals = Rp 523.000,00 - Rp 250.000,00 - Rp 74.000,00
Charcoals = Rp 273.000,00 - Rp 74.000,00
Charcoals = Rp 199.000,00

b.
2 Cartons of milk = Rp 25.000,00
1 Carton of milk = Rp 25.000,00 ÷ 2 = Rp 12.500,00

c.
2 Boxes of cereal = Rp 148.000,00
1 Box of cereal = Rp 148.000,00 ÷ 2 = Rp 74.000,00

d.
Cost = Rp 600.000,00 - Rp 52.000,00 = Rp 548.000,00
