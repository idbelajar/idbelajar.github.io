The main characteristic of a narrative text is that it uses imaginative language in past tense to express ideas and emotion. Narrative text has a three-part structure including orientation, complication, and outcome. Narrative text tells a series of events chronologically in both fiction (comic, novels) and nonfiction (biographies, memoirs) forms. 14. how we use should continuous?​


Past Future Continuous Tense

Pengertian Past Future Continuous Tense

Past future continuous tense adalah suatu bentuk kata kerja untuk menyatakan aksi atau situasi imajiner yang sedang berlangsung apabila unreal condition-nya terpenuhi (present continuous conditional ~ conditional sentence type 2 dengan continuous tense).

Rumus Past Future Continuous Tense

Past future continuous tense dibentuk dengan auxiliary verb would atau should dan be, dan present participle (-ing). Secara umum future continuous tense umumnya hanya terjadi pada aksi berupa dynamic/action verb, tidak stative verb karena umumnya hanya dynamic verb yang memiliki bentuk continuous
