5. A train of mass 800 000 kg is slowing down. What acceleration is produced if the braking force is 1 400 000 N


From task
we know :
mass = m = 800.000 kg
F = 1.400.000 N

Problem : acceleration?

solved :
a = F / m
   = 1.400.000 / 800.000
   = 1,75 m/s²
