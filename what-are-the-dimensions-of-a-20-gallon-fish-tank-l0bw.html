14. Siva visited a fish farm last week to learn about different fish species. He saw an empty fish tank which has a base of 70 cm x 60 cm. A water pipe that runs at 2400 cm3 per minute was turned on to fill the tank for 77 minutes. When the water pipe was turned off, 12 600 cm3 of water is still needed to fill the tank completely. What is the height of the fish tank?


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex]V= 2400\cdot 77 + 12600[/tex]

[tex]V= 184800 + 12600[/tex]

[tex]V= 197400[/tex]

[tex]70 \cdot 60 \cdot h= 197400[/tex]

[tex]h= \frac{197400}{70\cdot 60}[/tex]

[tex]h= 47[/tex] cm

So the height of the fish tank is 47 cm.
