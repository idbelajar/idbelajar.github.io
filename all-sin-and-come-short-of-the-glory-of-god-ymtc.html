3. Kuis[+50]: (+) God has created the universe and all of the living human beings. (-) (?) /ngasal, report/


Jawaban:

(+) God has created the universe and all of the living human beings.

(-) God has not created the universe and all the living human beings.

(?) has God created the universe and all the living human beings?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan menggunakan bentuk kalimat Present Perfect Tense yang berarti adalah kombinasi gramatikal dari present tense dan aspek perfect yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan peristiwa masa lalu yang memiliki konsekuensi hingga masa sekarang.

Present Perfect Tense mengikuti formula:

(+) Subject + Have/Has + Verb-3 + Object

(-) Subject + Have/Has + not + Verb-3 + Object

(?) Have/Has + Subject + Verb-3 + Object + ?

Semoga membantu ya.

≡ Present Perfect ≡

(+) God has created the universe and all of the living human beings.

(-) God hasn't created the universe and all of the living human beings.

(?) Has God created the universe and all of thhe living human beings?

Pendahuluan

Present perfect tense adalah jenis kalimat yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang bermula dari masa lalu namun masih berlanjut/berhubungan hingga sekarang.

rumus:

(+) subject + have/has + verb 3 + O/C

(-) subject + have/has + not + verb 3 + O/C

(?) have/has + subject + verb 3 + O/C + ?

contoh:

(+) I have talked to my parents about the visit.

(-) I haven't talked to my parents about the visit.

(?) Have I talked to my parents about the visit?

PEMBAHASAN

Ubah kalimat berikut ke bentuk negatif dan introgatif!

(+) God has created the universe and all of the living human beings.

(-) ...

(?) ...

Untuk mengubah ke bentuk kalimat negatif, beri 'not' setelah have/has.

➥ God hasn't created the universe and all of the living human beings.

Untuk mengubah ke bentuk kalimat introgatif, pindahkan have/has ke awal kalimat. Lantas beri tanda tanya di akhir kalimatnya.

➥ Has God created the universe and all of the living human beings?

4. what is the different of notice, short message and warning? and write the example of them! minimum 5 of each.


Jawaban:

Notice is one type of short functional text that contains a short and simple information in the form of text / images / signs / symbols to provide information, instructions or warnings to the public and usually in the form of signs.

ex :

1. notice ; Lab coats must be worn in this area

2. notice ;do not park here

3. notice ; This car park is under CCTV surveillance

4. notice ; keep doors closed all times

5. notice ; no food no drink allowed

Short message in Indonesian means "short message", which is a short text that is sent to tell someone about something or news that is important, also to order to do something (to inform someone about something or ask someone to do something).

ex :

1. send whattsapp chat with your friends

2. "Mum i went home tomorrow"

3. "Andy please come to the post office"

4. "send the subject to this email"

5."submit the project to your president class"

Warning is one type of short functional text that contains information in the form of warnings or directives that are shown to many people and usually associated with something that is quite dangerous.

ex :

1. warning ; do not enter

2. warning ;  Venomous snakes are observed / seen in this area

3. warning ; Laser radiation, don't look at the beam

4. warning ; high voltage

5. warning ; disruptive technology zone
