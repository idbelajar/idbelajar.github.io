11. apa perbedaan due the fact that,because,because of,dan due to?


1.Due to the fact that : Harus diikuti klausa
2.Because : Menghubungkan induk kalimat dan anak kalimat dengan menggunakan klausa keterangan diikuti subjek dan kata kerja
2.Because of : Menerangkan kata kerja
3.Due to : Menerangkan kata benda dan biasanya diikuti oleh verb to bedue the fact that diikuti oleh clause (main clause) sedang due to diikuti oleh noun phrase:
Due the fact that more tourists come to Bali, Bali gets more income this year.
Due to an increase in domestic and foreign tourists, Bali gets much more income.

Demikian halnya Because of dan because. Because of diikuti oleh noun phrase dan because diikuti olen main clause
