Jawaban:

A free market economy is one where the prices of goods and services are not regulated by the government and businesses that form are private entities who keep what they earn. The free market is the most efficient system to allocate scarce resources to a population.

10. The black economy is all economic activity in a given economy that occurs outside or in violation of the prevailing laws and regulations of society.activity in the black economy is often illegal, usually untaxed, and rarely recorded by official economic statistics. What problems will arise if black economy occured in a country ?


Jawaban:

People operate in the black economy to trade contraband, evade taxes and regulations, or evade price controls or rationing. A black economy usually arises when governments restrict economic activity to certain goods and services, either by making illegal transactions or by taxing those goods in such a way as to. Black markets may arise to provide illegal goods and services or to make expensive goods available at lower prices (such as pirated music or software).

As an example of the black economy, a construction worker who is paid below the table will not be taxed, nor will the employer pay taxes on his or her income.

Jawaban:

What problems will arise if black economy occurred in a country ?

The Problems that will arise if black economy occurred in a country are fraudulence, the possibility of violence, and being saddled with counterfeit goods or adulterated products, which is especially dangerous in the case of medications. Those problems will also lead to the inability of the government to accurately measure​ GDP, businesses will not be able to correctly gauge the​ market, and the government will be unable to design efficient tax policies. If all those problems happened, the real economy would collapse.

Penjelasan:

Apakah masalah yang akan muncul jika black economy terjadi di suatu negara?

Masalah yang akan muncul jika black economy terjadi di suatu negara adalah penipuan, kemungkinan kekerasan, dan dibebani dengan barang palsu atau produk palsu, yang sangat berbahaya dalam hal obat-obatan. Masalah-masalah tersebut juga akan menyebabkan ketidakmampuan pemerintah untuk mengukur pendapatan negara secara akurat, bisnis tidak akan dapat mengukur pasar dengan benar, dan pemerintah tidak akan mampu merancang kebijakan pajak yang efisien. Jika semua masalah itu terjadi, ekonomi yang sebenarnya akan runtuh.

The US economy is supported by abundant natural resources in the form of mineral resources, fertile soil, and a fairly long coastline in the Atlantic and Pacific Ocean regions.
