1. An alloy of silver and gold weight 15 ounces in air and 14 ounces in water. Assumming that silver loses 1/10 of its weight in water and that gold loses 1/19 of its weight, how many ounces of each metal are in the alloy?


Jawab:

the weight of each metal is

gold = 10 ⁵ / ₉ ounce

silver = 4 ⁴ / ₉ ounce

Penjelasan soal:

Soal tersebut menjelaskan bahwa

Terdapat campuran emas dan logamJika di udara 15 ons di air 14 onsJika di air emas kehilangan ¹/₁₉ beratnya dan perak kehilangan ¹/₁₀ beratnya

Pembahasan soal:

karena berat asli campuran logam tersebut adalah 15 ons, maka dapat ditulis dengan

[tex]\boxed {emas \ + \ perak \ = \ 15 \ ons}[/tex]

Lalu saya misalkan,

emas = a

perak = b

[tex]\boxed { a+b = 15 }[/tex]

di sini saya akan menggunakan metode eliminasi

a+b = 15

lalu kita masukkan persamaan tersebut menjadi

[tex]\frac{19-1}{19} a + \frac{10-1}{10} = 14\\\\\ \frac{18}{19}a + \frac{9}{10}b = 14 ---> (\times 190) \\\\\frac{18 \times 190}{19} a + \frac{9 \times 190}{10} b = 14 \times 190\\\\\ 180a+171b = 2660[/tex]

lalu kita samakan vaiabel a agar dapat di eliminasi

a+b = 15 ---> ( ×180)

180a + 180b = 2700

Maka,

180a + 180b = 2700

180a + 171b = 2660

10. A pot contained twice as much water as a bottle. The total amount of water in both the pot and the bottle was 5 l. What was the amount of the water in the bottle?​


Jawaban:

5/3 liters of water

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

pot = 2 × bottle

pot + bottle = 5 liters

substitute pot with x and bottle with y.

x = 2y

x + y = 5

substitute x with 2y into the equation.

x + y = 5

2y + y = 5

3y = 5

y = 5/3 liters

the amount of water inside the bottle was 5/3 liters.

x = 2y

x = 2(5/3)

x = 10/3

the amount of water inside the pot was 10/3 liters.
