3. What do we add to the end of regular verbs in the present perfect?


Jawaban:

present/past perfect , the regular verbs must be in 3 form or past participle. Usually we add -ed at the end of the word , but it's depend with the word given.

For example : Sing in past participle is Sung (you can't use -ed with this word, 'Singed' that sounds weird. )

Cook in past participle is Cooked

Ask in past participle is Asked

Penjelasan:

Simple present digunakan untuk peristiwa yang terjadi berulang kali/ secara rutin. Present continuous digunakan untuk kejadian yang sedang berlangsung detik ini juga

Formula Simple Present: Subject + Verb 1

Time signal Simple Present: Every... This... always

Formula Present Continuous: Subject + Verb ing

Time signal Present Continuous: Now, Currently, At the moment, Right now

Jika soal seperti nomor 10, (be) itu diganti dengan to be yaitu is/am/are. Tinggal lihat subjek untuk menentukan to be nya
