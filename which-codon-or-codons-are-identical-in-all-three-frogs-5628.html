Jawaban:

1. Why are the frogs called red-eyed tree frogs?

Because these frogs have beautiful colours and various kinds like trees, grass or wood. those colours could help them from other animals.

2. How does the colour green protect the frogs?

The green colour on their bodies helps them to camouflage and become a tree or grass.

3. Why do the frogs show all of their colours?

The frogs will show all their colours when there is an animal that wants to eat them like a snake. The bright colour will surprise the snake and create an opportunity for the frog to escape

4. What parts of a red-eyed tree frog are orange?​

the orange colour on the frog's body helps in camouflage. the orange and other colours on its body will help it camouflage on trees or leaves
