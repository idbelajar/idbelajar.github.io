13. The car id expansive it id (him / his)​


PART : PRONOUN-POSSESSIVE PRONOUN-PART OF SPEECH

----------------

ANSWER :

The car is expensive. It is his.

NOTE / DISCUSSION :

The correct one is "his" to replace the blanks. The word "his" states the possessive pronoun.  It means that "the car belongs to someone { him/ the man who owns that car } . We can't apply "him" to fill the blank because the word "him" as  an object.

Example :

I meet him. I know him.

That is his motorcycle. That motorcycle is his. He owns that motorcycle.

Analysis :

The word "him" as an object after the verb.

The word "his" as possessive adjective if it is followed by noun. Meanwhile, if the noun { motorcycle } precedes the pronoun "his" , it is as possessive pronoun. It means that he owns that motorcycle.

EXPLANATION :

PRONOUN

Pronoun is a word used to replace nouns. Pronoun has the function to prevent the repetition nouns in the sentence. Pronoun can be the subject, object, possession and it is based on the context of the sentence.

Pronoun merupakan kata ganti orang , baik kata ganti orang , benda dan suatu hal. Kata ganti orang (Pronoun ) dalam bahasa inggris mengalami perubahan ataupun tetap berdasarkan posisi part of speech (kelas kata ) dalam kalimat. Maka daripada itu , diharapkan memahami betul dari masing-masing fungsi  kelas kata dalam suatu kalimat, apakah termasuk subject (subyek), verb(kata kerja), object(obyek), object of preposition (objek preposisi ) , adjective (kata sifat) , preposition (kata penghubung) dan adverb (keterangan).

Example :

Mrs. Dee has a car. She loves it.

Analysis :

Kalimat diatas , kata "she" { dia perempuan } merupakan subjective pronoun { kata ganti orang yang berfungsi sebagai subyek } dan menggantikan  nama orang "Mrs. Dee" . Sedangkan kata "it" merupakan objective pronoun { Kata ganti benda yang berfungsi sebagai obyek } dan menggantikan sebuah benda "car."
