2. A picture frame is 9 inches long and 6 inches wide. It can display a picture 6 inches long and 4 inches wide. What is the area of the picture frame?


Area of Frame and Picture

Area = L * W

L = 9 

W = 6

Area = 9*6 = 54 in²

Area of Picture

Area = L*W

L = 6

W = 4

Area = 6 * 4

Area = 24 in²

Area of Frame

Area of Frame = Total Area - Picture Area

Area of Frame = 54 - 24

Area of Frame = 30 in²

9. A plan of a building is drawn on the scale of 1:200. a.) The width of the building is 15 m, how wide is the plan of the building? Give your answer in centimetres. b.) The length of the building drawn in the plan is 12 cm, how long the actual length oh the building? Give your answer in metres.


Jawaban:

a. 7½cm

b. 24m

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The scale for this plan is 1 : 200.

This means that 1 cm on the plan represents 200 cm (or 2 m) in real life.

a. actual width = 15m = 1500cm

width on a plan =

[tex] {1500cm} \times ({1 \div 200} ) \\ = 1500cm \times \frac{1}{200} =7 \frac{1}{2} cm[/tex]

b. length on a plan = 12 cm = 0,12m

actual length =

[tex] {0.12m} \div ({1 \div 200} ) \\ = 0.12m \times {200} =24m[/tex]

13. Fatima is wrapping a gift box for her nephew’s birthday. The box’s dimensions are 16 inches long by 10 inches wide by 5 inches high. How much paper was used to wrap the box?


Jawab:

580 square inches

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Given that a box has:

-- Length (a) = 16"

-- Breadth (b) = 10"

-- Height (c) = 5"

Find the surface area!

Surface area equation (box) =

2×(a×(b+c)+(b×c)) =

2×(16×(10+5)+(10×5)) =

2×((16×15)+50) =

2×(240+50) =

2×290 =

580 square inches

580 square inches of paper is needed to wrap the box
