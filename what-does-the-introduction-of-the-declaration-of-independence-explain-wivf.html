1. What is self introduction?

A self-introduction explains who you are, what you do and what others need to know about you. You should provide a self-introduction any time you meet someone new and don't have a third party to introduce you. Offer a self-introduction when you are: Beginning an interview.

Atau simplenya introduction bisa kita artikan sebagai "Perkenalan".

2. What are the expressions of self introduction (formal)?

- I would like to introduce myself. My name is…

- May I introduce myself. My name is…

- Let me introduce myself. My name is…

- Would you mind if I introduce myself. My name is…

Perkenalan (introducing) formal ini biasanya digunakan pada acara atau forum resmi, seperti perkenalan pada acara seminar dimana diacara tersebut terdapat tokoh atau orang penting

3. What are the expressions of self introduction (informal)?

- Hi, Good morning. I’m Shasya from Jakarta

- Hey bro/sis, how do you do? I’m Aulia, nice to see you.

Perkenalan (introducing) formal ini biasanya digunakan pada acara atau forum tidak resmi, seperti perkenalan pada saat ditempat umum bertemu dengan saudara teman atau teman baru. 

4. What are the responses of self introduction (formal)?

- I am very pleased to meet you.

- Glad to meet you too.

5. What are the responses of self introduction (informal)?

- I'm so happy to talk with you

- Lovely to meet you too

Answer: 

In any kind of writing, you always need to write introduction. It's the essential part of the writing. The main purpose of introduction is mainly to state the reason for you to write, normally on business letters like inquiry, complaint or confirmation. The other functions of it is to give a glimpse of issue based on our point of view, for essay or articles. Meanwhile, in a story or narrative, introduction is for stating the setting or the characters in it. So, it can be various for its main purpose depending on the kind of writing you make.

12. 2. Explain the meaning ofa Introductionb Body of letter​


Jawaban:

An introduction refers to a beginning — maybe presenting someone new to a group, or inserting a new idea into a project. ... The noun introduction comes from the Latin verb introducere, meaning "to lead in." This “leading in” might be the formal introduction of a speaker, or the speaker's own introduction to his remarks.

The body of the letter is the main and largest part of a letter. The body is made up of one or more paragraphs in which the main idea of the letter is relayed. The first paragraph of the body of a letter should include the reason for writing. Additional paragraphs should go into more detail about the subject.
