c. Nitric acid produces nitric salts

13. What is the value of n in the equation 64 ÷ n = 8


Jawaban:

[tex]64 \div n = 8[/tex]

[tex] \frac{64}{n} = 8[/tex]

[tex]64 = 8n[/tex]

[tex]8n = 64[/tex]

[tex]8n \div 8 = 64 \div 8[/tex]

[tex]n = 8[/tex]
