Light Laying Chicken Type This type of chicken is called white laying hens. This lightweight laying hens has a slim body, small and shining eyes. Its fur is pure white and has a red comb. This chicken comes from a pure white leghorn strain. This strain of chicken is hard to find, but commercial light laying hens are widely sold in Indonesia under various names. Medium Laying Chicken Type The body weight of this chicken is quite heavy. Even so, its weight is still between the weight of light layer chickens and broiler chickens. Therefore this chicken is called the medium layer type. This chicken body is not skinny, but also does not look fat. The eggs are quite a lot and can also produce a lot of meat. This chicken is also known as a dual-purpose type of chicken. Because of its brown color, this chicken is called brown laying hens, which generally have brown feathers as well. In the market, people say that brown eggs are preferred over white eggs, if you look at the color of the skin, the brown one is more attractive than the white one, but in terms of nutrition and taste, they are relatively the same. One thing that is different is the price in the market, the price of brown eggs is more expensive than white eggs. This is because brown eggs are heavier than white eggs and brown eggs produce less than white eggs. In addition, meat from medium layer chickens will be more salable to be sold as broilers with good taste.

3. Kondisi yang mempengaruhi produksi telur ayam petelur ringan adalah


Kondisi yang mempengaruhi produksi dari telur ayam petelur ringan antara lain adalah penyinaran, luas kandang ayam, kualitas pakan yang diberikan, kualitas induk ayam, kondisi lingkungan di sekitar kandang dan lain sebagainya. Kondisi-kondisi ini harus diperhatikan dengan baik untuk meningkatkan produksi telur ayam.

Pembahasan

Jika ingin meningkatkan produksi telur ayam maka kita harus memperhatikan sejumlah aspek seperti penyinaran. Lama penyinaran sekitar 16 jam dan bisa menggunakan lampu atau matahari. Selain itu, luas kandang turut mempengaruhi produksi telur sebab kandang yang lebih leluasa akan meningkatkan jumlah telur yang dihasilkan. Selain dua hal tadi, hal lain yang mempengaruhi produksi telur adalah kualitas pakan dan induk petelur. Jika kualitas keduanya baik maka produksi telur bisa ditingkatkan.
