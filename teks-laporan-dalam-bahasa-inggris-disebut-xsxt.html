7. teks laporan bahasa inggris tentang kejadian semeru kemarin​


Jawaban:

JAKARTA - Emergency handling after the hot cloud avalanche of Mount Semeru is still ongoing on the fourth day. The eruption disaster not only had an impact on the loss of life and damage, but also for residents who were displaced due to the damage to their homes due to volcanic materials.

The latest data from the Command Post (Posko) for Disaster Emergency Response Impact of Hot Clouds and Mount Semeru Falls today, Tuesday (7/12), at 12.00 WIB, the number of displaced residents has increased to 3,697 people. Most of the displaced residents were in Lumajang Regency, while in Malang Regency there were only 24 people.

The distribution of evacuation points in Lumajang Regency is in Pronojiwo District with 9 points totaling 382 people, Candipuro District 6 points 1,136 people, Pasirian District 4 points 563 people, Lumajang District 188 people, Tempeh District 290 people, Sumberseko District 67 people, Sukodono District 45 people ,

Data on the death toll recorded that 56 people were injured, 17 people were missing and 34 people died, while the total affected population was 5,205 people. Regarding the number of residents who were declared missing and injured, the Command Post is still updating the data and validating it.

In addition to the impact of fatalities, the eruption resulted in 2,970 housing units being affected. The local government is still updating the number of houses affected and the level of damage. Other affected buildings are 38 educational facilities and 1 unit broken bridge (Gladak Perak).

On the fourth day after the eruption, President Joko Widodo visited the affected locations in Lumajang Regency. The President arrived at the Sumberwuluh Village Field, Lumajang Regency, at 10.21 WIB. President Jokowi met with survivors, saw public kitchens and inspected the Health post and handed over compensation to the heirs of the victims who died as a result of the eruption.

Meanwhile, Mount Semeru was observed to experience 2 earthquake eruptions and the duration of the earthquake was 55 – 125 seconds. In addition, the Center for Volcanology and Geological Hazard Mitigation (PVMBG) informed that there were 7 avalanches of earthquakes with a duration of 50 – 120 seconds. Regarding the PVMBG recommendations for the volcanic activity of Mount Semeru as follows.

First, people are not active within a radius of 1 km from the crater or peak of Mount Semeru and a distance of 5 km to the direction of the crater opening in the southeast - south sector, and are wary of hot clouds avalanche, lava avalanches and lahars along rivers or valleys that originate at the peak of Mount Semeru. . Furthermore, the radius and distance of this recommendation will be evaluated continuously to anticipate if there are symptoms of changes in hazard threats.

Second, people should stay away from or not move in areas affected by hot cloud material because the temperature is still high.

Third, the public needs to be aware of the potential for slides along the Besuk Kobokan hot cloud path valley.

Jawaban:

Coronavirus Desease 2019

or abbreviated Covid 19 is a infectious diseases which is caused by SARS-CoV-2, one of type coronavirus. This disease first detected in Desember 2019 in Wuhan city, China. This disease initially exist in animals but, because there serving food whose animals are still alive so, that’s virus contagious to humans. To date 197.168 people are infected covid 19 in the world.

Symptoms of this disease are almost the same as the symptoms of flu, but there are different ,among them fever, dry cough, fatigue, sputum production, shortness of breath, muscle / joint pain, sore throat, headache, chills, nausea / vomiting, nasal congestion, diarrhea, coughing up blood, and conjunctival congestion. These symptoms are notified by the World Health Organization (WHO). The incubation period for this virus according to WHO for 1-14 days while according to the CDC for 2-14 days. This virus can survive in inanimate objects for 9 days if the ambient temperature is no more than 30 ° C and this virus cannot survive in the air.

Covid 19 has a lot to infect well-known figures such as NBA athletes from a Utah club named Rudy Gobert, then there is an Arsenal football club coach named Mikel Arteta. Both have been tested positive for corona. Covid 19 has had a huge impact starting with the sports league being suspended, tourist attractions such as Disney Land have been temporarily closed, the Kaaba at the Grand Mosque has become empty, some music shows have been postponed, even schools have been temporarily closed.

The Indonesian government urges the public not to leave the house if it is not too important and to avoid crowds. Many medical staff are overwhelmed and hospital capacity is insufficient to accommodate patients. Our task is to reduce the epidemic curve so that it does not surge too much. In a way:

1) Don't leave the house

2) Avoiding crowds

3) Wash your hands frequently

4) Avoid physical contact with others

5) Limit travel

6) Avoid touching eyes, nose and mouth

7) Sneeze and cough with a tissue and throw it away

8) Do not hoard and increase the price of masks and hand sanitizers

WHO recommends using masks for people who have the flu and cough so they don't spread the virus.
