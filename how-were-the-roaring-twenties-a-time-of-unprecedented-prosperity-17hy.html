2. Lintang's apartment is located on the seventh floor of a ... building. (A) Twenty-story (B) Twenty-stories (C) Twenties-stories (D) Twenties-story


Lintang's apartment is located on the seventh floor of a (A) twenty-story building. Article 'a' menunjukkan bahwa frasa yang mengikuti adalah frasa kata benda, noun prhase. Noun phrase untuk sebuah bangunan berlantai dua-puluh adalah a twenty-story building (AmE) atau a twenty-storey building (BrE).

Pembahasan

Phrase (frasa dalam bahasa Indonesia) adalah kelompok kata untuk melengkapi kalimat. Frasa tidak memiliki subjek maupun objek. Oleh karena itu, frasa tidak bisa berdiri sendiri.

Berdasarkan fungsinya, phrase dibedakan menjadi:

Noun Phrase.Verb Phrase.Adjective Phrase.Adverb Phrase.Gerund Phrase.Infinitive Phrase.Absolute Phrase.

Noun Phrase adalah kumpulan kata yang bertindak sebagai sebuah kata benda (noun) dalam kalimat. Noun phrase digunakan untuk mengidentifikasi noun atau pronoun.

Contoh:

The black car in the garage is my car.My father is a kind-hearted man.They saw a black cat.

Beberapa noun phrase disusun dengan kata dasarnya saja, tidak diperlukan bentuk jamaknya. Contoh:

A five-year-old boy.A ten-story-building.

'Story-stories' digunakan dalam American English. 'Storey-storeys' digunakan dalam British English.

Terjemahan dari kalimat tersebut adalah "Apartemen Lintang terletak di lantai ke-tujuh dari sebuah bangunan yang berlantai dua puluh."

Jawaban:

1. An argumentative essay is a type of essay that presents arguments on both sides of a topic or issue. The purpose of this type of essay is to persuade the reader to accept a particular point of view or to take a specific action.

2. To write an argumentative essay, the following steps should be taken:

- Choose a topic that is debatable and has multiple perspectives

- Research and gather evidence to support your argument

- Create an outline for the essay, including an introduction, body paragraphs, and a conclusion

- Introduce the topic and present your thesis statement in the introduction

- Use evidence to support your argument in the body paragraphs

- Address any counterarguments and refute them in the body paragraphs

- Restate your thesis and summarize your main points in the conclusion

3. The connectors in the essay are:

- "however" - used to introduce a contrasting idea

- "despite" - used to introduce a contrasting idea

- "furthermore" - used to introduce additional supporting information

4. The topic of this essay is the role of women in the development of their countries.

5. Women contribute to the development of their countries in various ways, including by participating in the workforce, engaging in entrepreneurship, and participating in the political process.

6. The role of women in the prosperity of society is important and significant. Women bring unique perspectives and skills to the table, and their contributions should be recognized and valued.
