15. Help me please :A mathematics club consisted of 40 members. 70% of the members were girls. After some members joined the club, 60% of the members are girls. if the new members consist of 6 more boys than girls, how many members in total are boys.According to answer key, the answer should be 20 boys, but I do not get how to solve that.


Jawab:

20

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

let x = girls and y = boys

before new members joined :

girls --> x/40*100% = 70%

                            x = 28 --> so boys will be 40-28 = 12


after new members joined : let x' = new girls and y' = new boys

y' = x'+6



girls --> (28+x')/(40+x'+y')*100% = 60%

                      (28+x')/(40+x'+y') = 3/5

                                     5(28+x') = 3(40+x'+y')

                                      140+5x' = 120+3x'+3y'

                                        2x'-3y' = -20

                                  2x'-3(x'+6) = -20

                                   2x'-3x'-18 = -20

                                                x' = 2

                                               


so total boys are 12+2+6 = 20
