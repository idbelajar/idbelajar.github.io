B. Usually plays football in the fieldPembahasan :

SIMPLE PRESENT

Simple present adalah suatu bentuk kata kerja sederhana yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan kebiasaan, fakta, dan hal umum.

Rumus :  

1. Verbal Sentence :  Terdapat kata kerja

(+) S + V1 + O(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O(?) Do/does + S + V1 + O + ?

2. Nominal Sentence : Tidak terdapat kata kerja

(+) S + be (am, is, are) + Adj/Adv/Noun(-) S + be (am, is, are) + not + Adj/Adv/Noun(?) Be (am, is, are) + S + Adj/Adv/Noun + ?

- Penggunaan "to be" : am (I), (is ( Singular ), are ( Plural, you)

- Penggunaan do/does :  does (Singular), do (plural, i, you)

- Penambahan s/-es di V1 apabila subjek Singular

Ditanyakan :

Ragil : What does Andi often do on Sunday morning?

Tino : He ....... (choose the correct sentence to fill the blank)

Penjelasan :

A. Usually play football in the field

Kesalahan : play (Kurang penambahan s untuk subjek singular)

B. Usually plays football in the field

Benar Sesuai dengan struktur verbal sentence singular ( S + V1 + s/es + O)

C. Usually playing football in the field

Kesalahan : playing (Tidak digunakan -ing di Simple Present)

D. Usually played football in the field​

Kesalahan : played ( Tidak digunakan -ed di Simple Present)
