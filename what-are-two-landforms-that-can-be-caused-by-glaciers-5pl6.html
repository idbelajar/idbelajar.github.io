11. Floods are caused by physical and human actives. What are they ?


Answer: 

Floods are one of the common natural disasters in our surroundings. However, this catastrophe is mostly caused by human activities, mainly littering the water sewage and rivers. The trash from their daily activities can be a real issue, especially during the rainy season. The river and sewage are supposed to be used to flow the water. Unfortunately, the trash makes them stuck and eventually the water rises to the street and the houses. Another human activities which has the same role in causing flood is deforestation. This cutting-down-trees activities causes not only flood but also mudslide as the trees, which are supposed to be protecting the housing / settlement areas from the water flowing down from the hill, do not exist anymore and there the floods begins.

Earth's tilted axis causes the seasons. Throughout the year, different parts of Earth receive the Sun's most direct rays. So, when the North Pole tilts toward the Sun, it's summer in the Northern Hemisphere. And when the South Pole tilts toward the Sun, it's winter in the Northern Hemisphere.
