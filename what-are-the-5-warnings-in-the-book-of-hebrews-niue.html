7. 3. The page of mathematics book starts from 1. If there are 852 digits used in the book, then the number of the last page of the book is ....?​


Jawab:

﻿320 pages

Unit digits:

Page 1-9 = 9×1 = 9 digits

Tens digits:

Page 10-99 = 90×2 = 180 digits

The rest digits:

= 852 - (9+180)

= 663

The hundred digits:

= 663 ÷ 3

= 221 pages

The total pages:

= 99 + 221

= 320

Reading Comprehension

Answer these questions based on the text!

1. What is e book?

(Apa itu e-book?)

E-book is fiction or nonfinction text that is distributed and read in digital form.

(E-book adalah teks fiksi maupun non-fiksi yang disebarkan dan dibaca dalam bentuk digital.)

Answer is found in paragraph 1.

2. How is it distributed?

(Bagaimana e-book didistribusikan?)

It is distributed worldwide in digital form over the internet.

(E-book didistribusikan di seluruh dunia dalam bentuk digital melalui internet.)

Answer is found in paragraph 1.

3. How can we read e book?

(Bagaimana kita bisa membaca e-book?)

We can read e-book by a device that uses special software to display the text for reading.

(Kita bisa membaca e-book dengan sebuah alat yang menggunakan perangkat lunak khusus untuk menampilkan teks untuk dibaca.)

Answer is found in paragraph 2.

4. What are the advantages of e-book and e-book device?

(Apa saja keuntungan ebook dan alat e-book?)

They can hold the texts of many different books at one time, and allow the reader to select from various type sizes, search the text, and switch quickly between books.

(Mereka bisa menampung teks dari banyak buku yang berbeda di satu waktu, dan memperkenankan pembaca untuk memilih berbagai tipe ukuran, mencari teks, dan mengganti dari satu buku ke buku lainnya dengan cepat.)

Answer is found in paragraph 3.

5. What are the disadvantages of e-book and e-book device?

(Apa saja kerugian dari e-book dan alat e-book?)

The computer or e-book device is costly, consumer resistance to reading on electronics screens, and uncertainty about the business model, including illegal copying of texts.

(Komputer dan alat e-book mahal, daya tahan konsumen untuk membaca di depan komputer, dan ketidakpastian tentang merek usaha termasuk masalah penyalinan teks ilegal)

Answer is found in paragraph 4.
