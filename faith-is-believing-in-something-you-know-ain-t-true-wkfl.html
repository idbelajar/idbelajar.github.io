8. Something you can find in your classroom is a..... *​


There are many things in your classroom they are:

 

1. Desk: Not only students who got table and chair, but also teachers. Teacher’s table called as desk.

 

2. White board: A board that require markers to be written on. Teacher are usually writing on it.

 

3. Eraser: This tool can be used to erase marker in the white board or pencil in the paper.  

4. Projector: Nowadays, classroom are provided with projector to help teaching activity.  

5. Pencil: The most common writing tool for students.

 

6. Ballpoint pen: This type of pen is being used to write in the notebook for students.

 

7. Calendar: Usually a calendar can be found in a class.

 

8. Map: A diagram of the world or a smaller geographical area, commonly found in the classroom walls.  

9. Globe: A three-dimentional model of the earth. Very common to found in classroom.  

10. Clock: A tool where you can see when time is moving.
