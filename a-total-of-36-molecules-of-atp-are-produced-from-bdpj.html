12. determine the number of molecules in 25g of methane​


Jawaban:

1,5625 mol atau 1.5625 moles

Penjelasan:

rumus : [tex]mol = \frac{gram}{Mr}[/tex]cari Mr atau massa molekul relatif nya dulu.

ketahui bahwa methane atau metana itu rumus kimianya [tex]CH_4[/tex] jadi terdiri dari 1 atom C (carbon) dan 4 atom H (hydrogen).

Mr didapat dari penjumlahan Ar (massa atom relatif)

Ar C itu 12, sedangkan Ar H itu 1

Mr = Ar C + 4 × Ar H

Mr = 12 + 4 × 1 = 16

jalankan sesuai rumus

mol = 25 g ÷ 16 = 1,5625

jadi, jumlah molekul pada 25 gram metana adalah 1,5625.

15. How many grams are in 1.2 x 10²⁴ molecules of water?​


Answer

Mass of water = 36 grams

Explanation

Molecules of water is written in formula : H₂O,

to calculate the mass of the water, we should know about realtive molecular mass of water from periodic table :

Mr H₂O = 2 x Ar H + Ar O = 2 x 1 + 16 = 18 g/mole

From Avogadro number ==>

[tex]\boxed{mole\ of\ water\ = \frac{Molecules\ number\ of \ Water}{6,02\ .\ 10^{23} } }[/tex]

mole of water = 1.2 x 10²⁴ /(6.02 x 10²³) = 1.99 mole = 2 mole

Mass of water = mole of water x Mr water = 2 mole x 18 g/mole

= 36 grams of water
