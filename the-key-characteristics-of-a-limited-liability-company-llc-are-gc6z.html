What are the characteristics of the dolphins ?

Dolphins have smooth, rubbery skin and are usually colored in some mixture of black, white, and gray. They have two flippers, or fins, on their sides, as well as a triangular fin on the back. Like other whales, they have an insulating layer of blubber (fat) beneath the skin.

Dolphins can be found all over the world and in different environments. They like to blow bubbles, they are chatty animals and they are intelligent, like us.

15. what are the characteristics of molluscs?​


Jawaban:

Characteristic Features of Phylum Mollusca :

- They are bilaterally symmetrical.

- They are triploblastic, which three layers.

- They show organ system grade of organisation.

- The body is soft and unsegmented.

- Body is divisible into three regions – head, a visceral mass, and ventral foot.

- Body is covered by a mantle and shell.

Penjelasan:

Molluscs (also know as mollusks) are soft-bodied, bilaterally symmetrical, segmented, coelomate animals; usually shelled having a mantle, ventral foot, anterior head, and a dorsal visceral mass.
