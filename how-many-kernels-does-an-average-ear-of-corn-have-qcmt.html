1. 4 sacks of corn kernels are shown below.-what is their Average mass?-What is the total mass of corn kernels ?​


4 kernels = 15kg, 6kg, 10kg, 5kg

total mass of corn kernels

= 15+6+10+5

= 36kg

Average = total data/frequency

= (15+6+10+5)/4

= 36/4

= 9 kg

So, the average mass is 9kg and the total mass is 36kg
