3. change these tense below to be passive sentences : 1. shakespeare wrote that play​


PASSIVE VOICE

Change this sentence below to be passive sentences :

[Ubah kalimat di bawah menjadi kalimat pasif]

1. Shakespeare wrote that play

Jawaban

Bentuk pasif dari kalimat tersebut adalah:

That play was written by Shakespeare.

___________

Penjelasan

Untuk mengubahnya menjadi kalimat pasif, gunakan rumus berikut.

Subject + was/were + Verb 3 + by + Agent

[Rumus kalimat pasif 'simple past tense']

Gunakan kata kerja be bentuk 'was' karena subjeknya adalah subjek tunggal.

Ubah kata kerja 'wrote' (verb 2) menjadi kata kerja 'written' (verb 3).

That play(subject) was written(verb 3) by Shakespeare(agent)

Hilangkan kata di dalam kurung pada kalimat di atas, maka itulah jawabannya.

⇒ That play was written by Shakespeare.

Fear no more the heat ‘o the sun,

Nor the furious winter’s rages;

Thou they worldly task hast done,

Home art gone, and ta’en they wages:

Golden lads and girls all must

As chimney-sweepers, come to dust.

Fear no more the frown o’ the great,

Thou are past the tyrant’s stroke;

Care no more to clothe and eat;

To thee the reed is as the oak;

The sceptre, learning, physic, must

All follow this, and come to dust.

Fear no more, the lightning-flash,

Nor th’ all-dreaded thunder-stone;

Fear not slander, censure rash;

Thou hast finished joy and moan:
