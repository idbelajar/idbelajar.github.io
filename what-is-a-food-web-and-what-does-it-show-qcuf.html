9. Answer the following questions. 1. What do 'there is' and 'there are' mean in Indonesian? 2. What is 'there is' followed by? 3. What is 'there are' followed by? 4.What does 'there is' show? Show the example. 5. What does 'there are show? Show the example. ​


Jawaban:

1. there is and there are have a similar meaning in Indonesia. They means "ada" in Indonesia. The difference is, you use 'there is' when the first noun in the series is singular or non-count, and you use 'there are' When the first noun in the series is plural

2. 'There is' is usually followed by the letter 'a' and a singular noun (if it's a positive sentence)

ex: There is a bag under the table

3. 'There are' is usually followed by plural noun (if it's a positive sentence)

ex: There are some bananas in the other table.

4. There is a meaning "there"

And some always follow with a single object (single) or the number is only one

Singular nouns followed by the article a or an at the beginning of the word

Example :

a book, a car, a student, a pencil, a bag, a lamp, an umbrella, an apple, an eye, an hour .

sentence example :

There is a glass on table

There is 1 set of kitchen tool in the kitchen

There is a dress for your party on the bed!

There is an apple in refrigerator

There is an owl on the tree

2. There are means "there"

There are definitely always followed by objects whose number is plural or more than one .

Plural nouns are generally indicated by the ending s or es at the end of the word .

example :

books, students, pencils, cars, boxes, watches dan masih banyak lagi contoh lainnya.

However, please remember ...

that there are also irregular nouns. What this means is that not all objects are plural or more than one in number are marked with an s or es at the end of the word.

example :

children, mice, deer, geese dan lainnya.

sentence example :

There are some children in our classroom

There are three pictures on the wall

I think there are six cars outside

There are some boxes in the shop

There are five kinds of flowers in the garden
