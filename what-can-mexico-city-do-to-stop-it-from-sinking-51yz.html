11. change from passive 1. the 1986 earthquake in Mexico City killed many people​


~Hai, sobat cerdas!~

Menyusun kalimat aktif ke dalam bentuk pasif

Jawaban:

Many people in Mexico City was killed by the 1986's earthquake

Penjelasan:

To change active form into passive, look what's tense used on the sentence.

Killed is the past verb of kill

How to change:

O + was/were + V3 + by + S + ROS

Untuk merubah bentuk kalimat aktif ke pasif, coba perhatikan penggunaan tense-nya. Kata killed merupakan bentuk verba kedua dari kill

O = many peopleV3 = killedS = the 1986's earthquake

Jadi, cara merubahnya:

the 1986's earthquake

Pindahkan setelah by

in Mexico City

Berperan sebagai keterangan, pindahkan setelah obyek

killed

Ubah verba dua kill menjadi killed (tetap)

many people

Berperan sebagai obyek, pindahkan ke awal kalimat

Jadi, kalimat pasifnya:

Many people in Mexico City was killed by the 1986's earthquake ✔️
