1. One pint of milk is about 560 ml. Cinta has 3.5 pints of milk. How many litres of milk does Cinta have? Help pliss


Jawab:

1.96L

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

1 pint = 560ml

3.5 pints = 1960ml

1ml = 0.001L

1960ml = 1960 × 0.001

= 1.96L
