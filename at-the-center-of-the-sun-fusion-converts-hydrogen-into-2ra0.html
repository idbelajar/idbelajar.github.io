13. Carilah Kalimat Pasif Yang Terdapat Dalam Bacaan Berikut, Dan Tuliskanlah Kalimat Tersebut Serta Terjemahkan!The Sun is a star found at the center of the Solar System. At around 1,392,000 kilometres (865,000 miles) wide, the Sun's diameter is about 110 times wider than Earth's.Around 74% of the Sun's mass is made up of hydrogen. Helium makes up around 24% while heavier elements such as oxygen, carbon, iron and neon make up the remaining percentage. The Sun generates huge amounts of energy by combining hydrogen nuclei into helium. This process is called nuclear fusion.The Sun's surface temperature is around 5500 degrees Celsius (9941 degrees Fahrenheit). The Sun's core is around 13600000 degrees Celsius. The average distance from the Sun to the Earth is around 150 million kilometers. Light from the Sun reaches Earth in around 8 m


answer :

Passive sentence

1. The sun is a star found at the center of Solar system. (Matahari adalah sebuah bintang yang ditemukan di pusat tata surya)

2. Around 74% of the sun's mass is made up of hydrogen. (Sekitar 74% kandungan matahari terbuat dari hidrogen)

3. This process is called nuclear fusion. (Proses ini dinamakan percampuran/penggabungan nuklir *nuklir dan atom dicombine)

Penjelasan :

Passive voice sentence adalah kalimat bahasa inggris dimana verb menjadi subject dalam kalimat. Biasanya ditandai dengan kemunculan verb to be seperti is/am/are jika simple present, lalu kata kerja yg dipakai menggunakan kata kerja bentuk ketiga contohnya Found dari find-found-found.
