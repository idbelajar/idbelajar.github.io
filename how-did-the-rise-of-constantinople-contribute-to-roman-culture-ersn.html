Questions about B.J. Habibie

1. How long did Habibie stay in Germany?

(Berapa lama Habibie tinggal di Jerman?)

He stayed in Germany for 19 years.

(Dia tinggal di Jerman selama 19 tahun)

Pembahasan

Habibie pindah ke Jerman pada tahun 1955 (paragraf 1). Habibie diminta Presiden Suharto untuk kembali ke Indonesia pada tahun 1974 (paragraf 4). Jadi, Habibie tinggal di Jerman selama 1974 - 1955 = 19 tahun.

2. How long did Habibie hold the tenure as the state minister of research and technology of Republic of Indonesia?

(Berapa lama Habibie memegang masa jabatan sebagai Menteri Negara Penelitian dan Teknologi Republik Indonesia?)

He held the tenure as the state minister of research and technology of Republic of Indonesia for 20 years.

(Beliau memegang masa jabatan sebagai Menteri Negara Penelitian dan Teknologi Republik Indonesia selama 20 tahun.)

Pembahasan

"From 1978 to 1998, Habibie was repeatedly appointed as the minister of State for Researched and technology" (paragraph 4). Jadi, Habibie memegang masa jabatan sebagai menteri penelitian dan teknologi selama 1998 - 1978 = 20 tahun.

3. What values did Habibie contribute to the development of Indonesia?

(Apa nilai yang Habibie kontribusikan untuk perkembangan Indonesia?)

Indonesia made significant changes to its political system that expanded competition and freedom of speech.

(Indonesia membuat perubahan signifikan pada sistem politik yang memperluas kompetisi dan kebebasan berpendapat)

Pembahasan

Dalam paragraf 5, "Under Habibie, Indonesia made significant changes ....."  Hal ini merupakan perubahan besar yang peling menonjol bagi Indonesia karena kebebasan berpendapat lebih dihargai sejak saat itu sampai sekarang.

4. What achievements did Habibie gain in Germany?

(Apa prestasi yang Habibie capai di Jerman?)

He received a diploma degree in engineering in 1960 and a title Doktor der Ingenieurwissenschaften in 1965, was offered a position with Messerschmitt-Bolkow-Blohm (he developed theories) and worked for Messerschmitt on development of the Airbus A-300B aircraft.

(Beliau mendapatkan gelar diploma untuk teknik pada tahun 1960 dan gelar Doktor der Ingenieurwissenschaften pada tahun 1965, ditawari posisi dengan Messerschmitt-Bolkow-Blohm (di sana, beliau mengembangkan teori-teori fisika) dan bekerja untuk Messerschmitt mengenai pengembangan pesawat Airbus A-300B.)

Pembahasan

(Apa perubahan yang Habibie buat selama masa kepresidenanya?)

He changed the political system of Indonesia by expanding competition and freedom of speech. He lifted restriction on political parties and ended cencorship by dissolving the Information Ministry.

(Beliau mengubah sistem politik Indonesia dengan memperluas kompetisi dan kebebasan berpendapat. Beliau mencabut larangan tentang partai politik dan mengakhiri penyensoran dengan membubarkan Kementrian Informasi)

Biography text is a detailed description about person's life. Teks biografi adalah deskripsi detail mengenai kehidupan seseorang. Dalam biografi, ciri bahasa yang digunakan biasanya menggunakan simple past tense.

Simple Past Tense biasanya digunakan untuk menyatakan tindakan di masa lalu, dan telah selesai di masa lalu.

Penanda waktu yang digunakan: yesterday, last week, last month, two days ago, this morning, in 1970.

Bentuk simple past

    (+) Subject + verb 2  

    (-) Subject + did + NOT + verb 1  

    (?) Did + subject + verb 1 .... ?

She slept too late last night.I didn't see him yesterday.Did you take my umbrella?What did you do last weekend?When someone knocked the door, I was studying.Where were you last night?I didn't see him this morning.Last month, I bought a new car.

6. how did stalin rise to power and defeat trotsky


Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: Sejarah
Kategori: biografi tokoh
Pembahasan:

Stalin was able to rise as Lenin's successor and defeat his rival, Trotsky despite being less intelligent than him because of several factors such as:
- Stalin's pragmatic nature to make his ambition come true by taking any chances possible without considering idealism, something that Trostky clung on firmly.
- Stalin didn't mind building coalition with anyone, as long as it could lead to his ambition.
- Stalin's ability to manipulate the public in order to get their sympathy, such as naming the city Petrograd as Leningrad to gain more support from the people.
- A number of Trostsky's fatal mistake, which led to his failure to be Lenin's successor, such as, not coming to Lenin's funeral, attacking people who were the key of his plan, criticising Lenin in his personal exposed letter to his friend, despite a great recommendation from Lenin to himself as the candidate successor.

Such unfortunate events on Trotsky and Stalin's ability to turn things round for his own advantage were the key of Stalin's success to replace Lenin.

7. Chase, Mafia and TimeCold decided to contribute some money to buy a present for their mother. Chase agreed to contribute 30% of the cost of the present while Mafia agreed to contribute 40% of the remaining amount. The rest of the price of the present will be paid by TimeCold. However, on the shopping day, they realised the price of the item had increased by 25%. Chase paid $36 for his share in the end. a) What was the original price of the present? b) How much did TimeCold have to pay for the present in the end?

A.) 96$

B.) 36$

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Chase contributes 30%

30% = 36$

[tex]x[/tex] = 36$ [tex]: \frac{30}{100}[/tex]

[tex]x[/tex] = 36$ × [tex]\frac{100}{30}[/tex]

[tex]x[/tex] = [tex]\frac{3600}{30}[/tex]

[tex]x[/tex] = [tex]120[/tex]

([tex]x[/tex] is a scale)

Mafia contributes 40%

40% = ?$

40% × [tex]x[/tex] = ?$

[tex]\frac{40}{100}[/tex] × 120 = ?$

[tex]\frac{4800}{100}[/tex] = ?$

[tex]48[/tex] = ?$

40% = 48$

TimeCold paid the rest of the price (100% - 30% - 40%)

TimeCold contributes 30%

30% = 36$

The price of the present = 36$ + 36$ + 48$

                                         = 120$

So, to find the original price, we must divide increased price with the total percent of the increased price (100% + 25% = 125%)

Jadi, untuk mencari harga asli, kita harus membagi kenaikan harga dengan total persen kenaikan harga (100% + 25% = 125%)

The original price = 120$ : (100% + 25%)

                              = 120$ : [tex]\frac{125}{100}[/tex]

                              = 120$ × [tex]\frac{100}{125}[/tex]

                              = [tex]\frac{12000}{125}[/tex]$

                              = 96$

So, the original price was 96$.
