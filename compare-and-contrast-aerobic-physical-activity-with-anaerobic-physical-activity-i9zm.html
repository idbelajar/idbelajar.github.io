3. There are 3 different levels of physical activity. please name these 3 and explain about it


Jawaban:

The 3 levels of physical activity are Light, Moderate, and Vigorous. These levels are measured by the metabolic equivalent (METs). The effects of exercise are different at each intensity level.

Examples of Light Intensity Activities:  

Sleeping Writing or typing Walking 2.7 km/h

Examples of Moderate Intensity Activities:  

Bicycle Walking 4.8 km/h Home exercise

Examples of Vigorous Intensity Activities:  

Jogging Push up Rope jumping
