1. A woman exerts a pressure of 100N/cm2 when standing on the floor. If her weight is 500N what is the area of the floor she is standing on?


Jawaban:

0,2 cm²

Penjelasan:

P = F/A

P = pressure / tekanan (N/m² atau Pa)

F = Force / gaya (Newton)

A = area / luasan (cm²)

diketahui:

F = 500N

P = 100 N/cm²

100 N/cm² = 500 N ÷ A

A = 100 N/cm² / 500 N

A = 1/5 cm² = 0,2 cm²

The area of the floor that she stands on is 0,2 cm²

Jawaban:

the mass of the object on the earth = the mass of the object on the moon = 20 kg. Because mass is shows the amount of material in a material, weight is measure the force due to the effect of gravity on mass

Penjelasan:

the mass of the object on the earth = the mass of the object on the moon

known

[tex]w_{Earth} = 200 \: N\\g_{Earth} = 10 \: N/kg\\g_{moon} = 1,7 \: N/kg\\[/tex]

asked

[tex]m_{moon} = ?[/tex]

[tex]w_{Earth} = m \cdot g_{Earth}\\200 \: N = m \cdot 10 \: N/kg\\\frac{200 \: N}{10 \: N/kg} = m\\20 \: kg = m[/tex]

3. The message is not to give up the dream or get demotivated by other people's opinion.

4. The word ' you' here refers to other people who look down on us.

Penjelasan:

Here's the explanation for each answer:

The theme of the song becomes clear enough after you relate the title and what's in the lyric. 'Defying gravity' literally means 'to challenge gravity'. The nature of gravity is going down. So, no matter how much you weigh on earth, you will go down. But in the song, the singer tries to refuse the 'gravity' which is seen from the statement 'I'm through accepting limits' or 'I'm through with playing by the rules of someone else's game.' One is the master of his/ her own life, not others, not even the 'so-called nature'. The purpose of this song is quite obvious, as stated above.The message within this song is quite clear as well that one strives for their dream with all one has, without even caring for others, even the nature challenges him/her. In the song, it can be seen from the statement at the last sentence of the refrain, 'you won't bring me down.'The word 'you' can be taken metaphorically as the 'gravity' itself, which represents the nature, the rules which are not meant to be broken, or literally the other people even the world. The meaning of 'defying gravity' is the same as explained in number 1.
