9. Peter, Jamal and Khan were on their way to a resort. Peter drove 1/3 of the journey at a constant speed of 64 km/h. Jamal took over and drove 5/12 of the journey for 20 min ata constant speed of 60 km/h. Khan then took over and drove the remaining journey.ial How far did Jamal drive?[b] How many minutes did Peter drive?​


a). jamal time = 20 minutes = 20/60 hour = 1/3 hour

distance = speed x time

= 60 km/h x 1/3 h

= 20 km


b). 20 km = 5/12 of the journey

total distance = 12/5 x 20 km = 48 km

peter distance = 1/3 part of journey x 48 km = 16 km

peter time = distance/speed

= 16 km /64 km/h

= 1/4 hour

= 1/4 x 60 minutes

= 15 minutes
