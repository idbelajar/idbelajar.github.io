1. A game consists of black and white pieces. The number of black pieces is5 more than 3 times the white pieces. 7 white and 15 black pieces areremoved each round. After several rounds, there are 3 white and 56 blackpieces left. How many pieces were there in the beginning?​


Jawab:

If:

B = Total amount of black pieces

W = Total amount of white pieces

B = 3W + 5

Each round: W - 7, B - 15

After several rounds there are:

W = 3

B = 56

The calculation will be if we compare W with B:

Last round = 3 : 56

1st previous round = 10 : 71

2nd previous round = 17 : 86

3rd previous round = 24 : 101

4th previous round = 31 : 116

5th previous round = 38 : 131

6th previous round = 45 : 146

7th previous round = 52 : 161

Because on the 7th previous round B = 3W + 5 then the white and black pieces in the beginning is: 52 white pieces and 161 black pieces.

6. If we use a paper whith 2 colors white on one side and black on thae other side, and we throw it 9 times, 3 time it fell black and 6 times white! what are the chance that white color will fall if we throw it again .?


Jawaban:

the chance of the white color will fall is after 3 throws (kemungkinan warna putih akan jatuh setelah tiga lemparan)

Penjelasan:

disitu dijelaskan bahwa ada sebuah kertas dengan 2 warna, satu sisi adalah putih dan sisi lainnya adalah hitam. lalu dilempar 9 kali, dalam 3 lemparan kertas tersebut mendarat dengan warna hitam 3 kali dan dalam 6 lemparan selanjutnya warna putih mendarat 6 kali. lalu ditanya kesempatan warna putih akan jatuh.. maka jawabannya warna putih akan jatuh setelah 3 lemparan

polanya: 3 (hitam) -  6 (poutih) - 3 (hitam lagi) - 6 (putih lagi)

demikian seperti itulah kesempatan warna putih akan jatuh

translate inggris:

it explains that there is a paper with 2 colors, one side is white and the other side is black. then tossed 9 times, in 3 tosses the paper landed in black 3 times and in the next 6 throws the white landed 6 times. then asked about the chance that white will fall .. then the answer is white will fall after 3 throws

pattern: 3 (black) - 6 (white) - 3 (black again) - 6 (white again)

that's how the white chance will fall
