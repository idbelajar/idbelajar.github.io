5. change the sentences below into past tanse (+)! they walk to the office


Past Tense

(+) they walked to the office

Pembahasan Past Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang dilakukan di masa lalu atau sebuah Kegiatan yang terjadi di masa lampau.

Rumus Past Tense

(+) S + V2 + Complement

(-) S + did + not + V1 + Complement

(?) Did + S + V1 + Complement?

Kalimat Past Tense

(+) they walk to the office

(-) they did not walk to the office

(?) Did they walk to the office?

KESIMPULAN

Perintah soal “Change the sentences below into past tanse (+) they walk to the office”

=> Ubahlah kalimat tersebut menjadi Past Tense

=> Maka kata kerjanya menjadi Verb-2

Keterangan:Subject => TheyVerb-2 => walkedComplement => to the office

14. Mohon bngt bantu ya tp jgn ngsal.Exercises, CHANGE INTO INDIRECT !1. Maria said," My father died this morning".2. The gardener said," Don't walk on the grass."​​​​​


INDIRECT SPEECH

1) Maria said her father had died that morning.

2) The gardener forbade to walk on the grass.

EXPLANATION

Reported speech/indirect speech adalah kalimat berita, ungkapan melaporkan ulang apa yang telah disampaikan narasumber kepada orang lain tanpa mengubah informasi tersirat.

Hal² yang diperhatikan dalam merubah direct speech ke reported speech :

~ Perhatikan jenis kalimatnya, apakah termasuk afirmasi, tanya dan imperatif [saran, nasihat, larangan, perintah].

~ Perhatikan sudut pandangnya [point of view]

~ Perhatikan jenis reporting verb-nya; bentuk PAST [told, asked, warned] atau PRESENT [tell, ask, warn].

Jika menggunakan "Present simple, Present perfect, Future", noun clause verb tdk mengalami backshift.Sebaliknya, jika reporting verb berbentuk past {said, told, asked, warned}, noun clause verb mengalami backshift.

BASED ON YOUR QUESTIONS

(1)

Maria said, "My father died this morning."

➡ Maria said her father had died that morning.

Perubahan tenses pada noun clause verb mengikuti jenis kata pengiringnya "said." Awalnya menggunakan simple past menjadi past perfect.Keterangan waktu "this morning" mundur ke "that morning."

(2)

The gardener said," Don't walk on the grass."

➡ The gardener warned not to walk on the grass.

➡ The gardener forbade to walk on the grass.

Kalimat langsungnya berupa kalimat larangan, jadi untuk noun clause verb-nya dirubah ke bentuk infinitive.Kata pengiring juga perlu dirubah menggunakan "forbade" atau "warned" untuk menekankan konteks kalimatnya.
