3. In an acid base titration, 17,45 ml of 0,180M nitric acid HNO3 were completely neutralized by 14,76 ml of Al(OH)3 . calculate the concentration of the Al(OH)3


[HNO3] : 0,180 M
V HNO3 : 17,45 mL
[Al(OH)3] : ........ M ?
V Al(OH)3 : 14,76 mL

Answered

Step 1
Make a reaction of each compounds

Reaction
3HNO3 + Al(OH)3 → Al(NO3)3 + 3H2O

Step 2
Determine the number of mmole of HNO3

M : mmole / mL
mmole : M x mL

mmole HNO3 : M HNO3 x V. HNO3
mmole HNO3 : 0,180 mmole/mL . 17,45 mL
mmole HNO3 : 3,141 mmole

Step 3
Determine mmole of Al(OH)3 by using the coefficient reaction of Al(OH)3 toward the coefficient reaction of HNO3 based on the reaction above.

mmol Al(OH)3 : (c. Al(OH)3 / c. HNO3) x mmole HNO3
mmole Al(OH)3 : (1/3) x 3,141 mmole
mmole Al(OH)3 : 1,047 mmole

Step 4
Determine concentration of the Al(OH)3.

M : mmole / mL

M Al(OH)3 : 1,047 mmole / 14,76 mL
: 0,071 mmole / mL
: 0,071 M

So, the concentration of Al(OH)3 is 0,071 M

4. How is acid rain produced ?


Acid rain is sometimes referred to as acid deposition, a term that also encompasses other forms of acidic precipitation such as snow. It can occur as a result of both human and natural activities. Most of gases that lead to acid rain are typically by-products of burning fossil fuels and electric power generation. Erupting volcanoes also release chemicals that lead to acid deposition.

Acid rain leads to the acidification of streams and lakes, which directly endangers aquatic life. It also leads to the destruction of trees and sensitive forest soils. Acid deposition has negative impacts on architecture because it fastens the decay of paints and building materials. When the acid rain falls on a building, it begins to slowly react with the minerals in the stones, causing them to gradually disintegrate and eventually wash away. There are multiple ways to curb acid deposition, ranging from individual action to societal changes.

15. There are too much cars on the street. The word much is false. So it can be changed by.


Kalimat pada osal diatas mengandung Quantifier untuk countable nouns (benda yang dapat dihitung). Sehingga quantifier yang tepat digunakan adalah:

There are too many cars on the street

Pembahasan

Jadi, countable quantifiers adalah kata pembilang untuk menyatakan kuantitas atau jumlah dari kata benda (noun) yang dapat dihitung. Countable noun contohnya seperti chair, table, apple, glasses, dan lain-lain. Contoh quantifier yang dapat digunakan: many, few, large amount, etc

Uncountable quantifiers adalah kata atau frasa yang berlaku saat kita ingin menunjukkan jumlah dari noun yang tak dapat dihitung. Contoh uncountable noun adalah milk, water, sand, grass, rice, dan masih banyak lagi. Contoh quantifier yang dapat digunakan: much, little, a bit, etc
