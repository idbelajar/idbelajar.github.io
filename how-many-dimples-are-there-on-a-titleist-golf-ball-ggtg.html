9. There are 60 red balls,84 blue balls,and 72 green balls rina wants to put all ball with equal number(use hcf)how many box does she need


Jawaban :

So, 12 boxes are needed to put all balls with equal number.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

General Definition :

HCF (Highest Common Factor) is the largest number that divides two or more numbers.

To be known :

60 red balls

84 blue balls

72 green balls

To be asked :

How many boxes needed to put all balls with equal number ?

Working :

- Write down all prime factors for each numbers.

60 = 2 × 2 × 3 × 5 = 2² × 3 × 5

84 = 2 × 2 × 3 × 7 = 2² × 3 × 7

72 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 = 2³ × 3

- Find the highest common factors by taking the smallest prime numbers.

They are 2² × 3

Conclusion :

Hence, the HCF of 60, 84, and 72 is 2² × 3 = 4 × 3 = 12.
