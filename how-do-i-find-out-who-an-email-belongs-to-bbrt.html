2. I don't know how to create an email account. Can you tell me how to do it?


Ungkapan giving instruction biasanya pakai imperative sentence atau kalimat perintah yang bernada positif. Contoh:

Peel the carrots and cut into cube.

(Kupas wortel-wortelnya dan potong kotak-kotak.)

Wash the spinach then boil for 2 minutes.

(Cuci bayam lalu rebus selama 2 menit.)

Dalam percakapan, kita bisa menambahkan kata 'please' supaya bisa sopan.

Supaya berurutan, kita bisa pakai kata-kata berikut:

First, ...Second, ...Third, ...Then...Next...After that...Finally, ...

Contoh lengkap giving instruction:

How to cook instant noodles

1. First, boil 2 glasses of water in a pot.

2. Second, add noodles, breaking up if desired.

3. Then, cook for 3 minutes or until noodles are tender, stirring occasionally.

4. After that, remove from heat and stir in seasoning from flavor.

5. Finally, enjoy your noodle.

How many siblings do you have? Etc.) 3. What do you write to end your letter / email?


Here is Alia responding to an email that her penpal, Nakagawa that she knew from the internet.

1. To start responding an email, Alia should greet Nakagawa and have a little bit of small talk or chit-chat.

2. Start writing about details of Alia’s life. Age, school, hobbies, etc.

3. To end the email, you could write about your hope and wishes.


Let’s start writing the email:


Hi Nakagawa,


How are you? Hope all is well in your world.


Let me introduce myself. My name is Alia, I’m 16 years old girl from Indonesia. Currently I’m studying in Tunas Bangsa Senior High School in Jakarta at tenth grade. My hobbies are writing, swimming and hiking. I’m the only child in my family that’s why sometimes when I need someone to talk to, I’m going to penpal to look for new acquaintances.


What about you Nakagawa? Tell me about yourself and your family! I would like to read about it too :)


If you have some time in the future, I would like to visit you in Tokyo or maybe you could come to Jakarta? Hehe just kidding. Waiting for your reply!



Best,

Alia
