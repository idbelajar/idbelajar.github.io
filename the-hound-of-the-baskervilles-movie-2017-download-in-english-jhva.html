Arti dari "he was the best English News Reader in 2017" adalah

Dia adalah seorang pembaca berita Inggris terbaik pada tahun 2017.

Pembahasan

Jika diartikan per kata, hasilnya adalah seperti berikut.

He = Dia (Laki-laki)

was = adalah (Verb 2 dari is; menunjukkan masa lalu)

The Best = terbaik

English = Inggris

News = berita

Reader = Pembaca

Ingat posisi penulisan dalam B. Inggris, adjective (kata sifat), comparative (kata pembanding), dan superlative selalu diletakkan di depan noun (kata benda).

in = dalam [tahun] /di (tapi disini lebih cocok nenggunakan "pada tahun")

2017 = 2017 (year)

8. A piano cost $800 more in 2016 than in 2015. This was a 20% increase from the price in 2015. The price of the piano increased by 25% from 2016 to 2017. Find the cost of the piano a) in 2016 b) in 2017


The cost of the piano in 2016 was $4,800.The cost of tue piano in 2017 was $6,000.

In our daily life, we use percentages especially when it comes to money such as bank interest, discounts, salary increases, price increases for goods and so on. Percentage is a fraction that states the proportion of a number to the number 100 as a whole. It means, a fraction can be used as a percentage when the denominator is 100. A% of a number P is representing A/100 × P.

For more details, see the discussion of the following problems.

PEMBAHASAN :

• A piano cost $800 more in 2016 than in 2015. This was a 20% increase from the price in 2015.

If the pr of a piano in 2015 wss assumed to be P, then the right mathematical sentence to state the cost of the piano in 2016 is :

P + 20%P

or

P + $800

it means, 20%P = $800

P = $800 ÷ 20%

P = $800 × [100/20]

P = the cost of the piano in 2015 = $4,000

So, the cost of the piano in 2016 was $4,000 + $800 = $4,800.

• The price of the piano increased by 25% from 2016 to 2017. Then, the right mathematical sentence to state the cost of the piano in 2017 is : $4,800 + [25% × $4,800]

= $4,800 + $1,200

= $6,000

So, the cost of the piano in 2017 was $6,000.
