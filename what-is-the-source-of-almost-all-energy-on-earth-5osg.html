15. The gravitational force of a satellite on the surface of the Earth of radius R is F. What is the gravitational force on the satellite when its height is R above the Earth?​


Jawaban:

F/4

Penjelasan:

h = distance from earth core to satellite

h = R+R = 2R

Fb = gravitational force on the satellite when its height is R above the Earth

F = G m / R^2

Fb = GM m / (2R)2

Fb = GM m / 4R2

Fb = (GM m / R2) / 4

Fb = F/4
