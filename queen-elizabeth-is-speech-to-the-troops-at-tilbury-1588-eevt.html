Penghargaan: Order of the Star of Romania

12. 1.what is the topic of his speech2. What is Councillor steward trying to do to the audience in his speech?3. Why does he think that a new town hall is not necessary?4. So what is his condusion at the end of his speech?5. Identify which are the thesis, the arguments, and the reiteration.6. Does the speech take the form at analytical exposition? Why?​tolong banget dong kalau ada yang tau butuh bangett ​


The topic of Councilor Harper’s speech is to propose the construction of the new town hall. Councillor steward is trying to influence the audience to oppose the construction of the new town hall. He think that a new town hall is not necessary because it’s a form of wasting public fund money. His conclusion at the end of his speech is that the project will be a gross extravagance in the expenditure of public funds.

   5. The thesis of Harper’s speech is: the construction of the new town hall.

The arguments is: the construction will open a better place for the workers and society. Reiteration: the construction will be valuable to the public. Councillor Steward’s thesis: he is against the construction. Arguments: the construction will be a waste of public funds. Reiteration: the public funds will be better if is used for education and social services.

  6. Yes, the speech takes the form of analytical exposition because it has thesis, arguments and reiteration parts.

Pembahasan

Dua pidato di atas adalah tentang debat publik mengenai pro dan kontra pembangunan kembali balai kota (the construction of the town hall). Yang pro terhadap pembangunan tersebut adalah coucillor (anggota dewan) Harper. Dia mengajukan arguments bahwa pembangunan tersebut akan bermanfaat bagi publik untuk menyediakan tempat kerja yang baru bagi publik. Di sisi lain councillor Steward menentang pembangunan tersebut dengan alasan bahwa itu akan menjadi pemborosan uang publik yang akan lebih bermanfaat bila dipakai untuk pendidikan dan layanan sosial.
