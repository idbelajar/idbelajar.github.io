10. 1. a box in the shape of a cube with a volume of 166,375. what is the length of the side and calculate the circumference2. a ball has a radius of 15 cm, what is its volume and circumference?​


Jawaban:

1.) a. S = 55 unit

b. K = 660 unit

2.) a. V = 14.130 cm³

b. K = 942 cm

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

1.) Question:

a box in the shape of a cube with a volume of 166,375. what is the length of the side and calculate the circumference?

Translation:

Sebuah kotak berbentuk kubus dengan volume 166.375. Berapa panjang sisinya dan hitung kelilingnya?

A. Panjang sisi / side length:

S = ³√Volume

S = ³√166.375

S = 55 unit

B. Keliling / Around:

K = 12 × S

K = 12 × 55

K = 660 unit

2.) Question:

a ball has a radius of 15 cm, what is its volume and circumference?

Translation:

sebuah bola memiliki jari-jari 15 cm, berapa volume dan kelilingnya?

A. Volume:

V = 4/3 × π × r³

V = 4/3 × 3,14 × 15³

V = 4/3 × 3,14 × 3.375

V = 4/3 × 10.597,5

V = 14.130 cm³

B. Keliling / Around:

K = 4/3 × π × r²

K = 4/3 × 3,14 × 15 × 15

K = 4/3 × 706,5

K = 942 cm
