Given that a pond has:

depth (d) of 3 metresbase area (a) of 40 m²base perimeter (p) of 242 m

Find the surface area of water!

The surface area of water =

twice the base area (for the top surface of water and the base of water) +

the area of the outer body =

2×a + p×d =

2×40 + 242×3 =

80 + 726 =

806 m²

Therefore the surface area of water is 806 square metres.

10. A right circular cylinder has a base of radius 14 cm and height 21 cm. Find:-(i) The Volume of the cylinder.(ii) Area of the curved surface._____________Note:- Need the solution as well as explanation in English. :)​


Cylinder volume = 12.936 cm³Cylinder surface area = 3.080 cm³

Preliminary

The tube (cylinder) is a shape that has a base and a circular base. The properties of the tube are as follows :

Has 2 sidesHas no corner point Has a curved plane

Completion Be discoveredRadius (r) = 14 cmHeight (h) = 21 cmAskedVolume Surface areaAnswer

(ⅰ) Volume = πr²h

= 22/7 × 14² × 21

= 22/7 × 196 × 21

= 22 × 28 × 21

= 12.936 cm³

(ⅱ) Surface area = 2πr (r + h)

= 2 × 22/7 × 14 × (14 + 21)

= 2 × 22 × 2 × (14 + 21)

= 88 × 35

= 3.080 cm²

❍ In this Question, It is concluded that circular cylinder has a base of radius 14cm and the concluded height is 21cm..

First, We will find out the the volume of cylinder by using the appropriate formula of cylinder.. Also, we will take pi as 22/7 because it is not mention that which value of formula we have to choose..

Where -

r = radiush = height

[tex]\begin{gathered}\dashrightarrow\sf{Volume = \pi \times {r}^{2} \times height}\\\\\\ \dashrightarrow\sf{Volume = \dfrac{22}{\cancel{7}}\times \cancel {14} \times 14 \times 21}\\\\\\ \dashrightarrow \sf{Volume \: = 22 \times 14 \times 21} \\\\\\ \dashrightarrow \underline {\sf {\pink{Volume \: = 12.936 \:{cm}^{3} }}}\end{gathered}[/tex]
