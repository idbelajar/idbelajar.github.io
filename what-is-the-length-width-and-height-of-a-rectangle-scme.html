4. A rectangle has a perimeter of 42 meters and a length of 13 meters. Find the width of the rectangle.


The width of the rectangle is 8 meters.

[tex]\\[/tex]

Pembahasan

(Explanation)

The formula of the perimeter of a rectangle is p = length + width + length + width. It can be simplified to p = 2(length + width) or p = 2(l + w). As we know the length of the rectangle and the formula of the perimeter of a rectangle, we can use the formula to find the width of the rectangle as follows.

⇒   p = 2(l + w)

⇒   42 = 2(13 + w)

⇒   42 = 2(13) + 2(w)

⇒   42 = 26 + 2w  

⇒   -2w = 26 - 42  

⇒   -2w = -16

⇒   w = -16/-2

⇒   w = 8

Therefore, the width of the rectangle is 8 meters.

5. The perimeter of a rectangle is 84 cm. The length of a rectangle is 5 cm more than 3 timesthe width. a. Find the mathematical model b. Determine length and width c. Determine the area of a rectanglePlease Ans​


Pembahasan

Persegi panjang adalah bangun datar yang memiliki 4 sisi (dua pasang sisi sama panjang), 4 titik sudut, 4 sudut siku-siku di setiap sudutnya.

Luas persegi panjang merupakan perkalian dari panjang dan lebar persegi panjang. Luas persegi panjang dinotasikan dengan :

[tex]\boxed{A_{\text{rectangle}} = p \times l} \\ \\ [/tex]

p adalah panjang sisi persegi panjang, sedangkan l adalah lebar persegi panjang.

Keliling persegi panjangnya adalah dua kali jumlah panjang dan lebar sisi persegi panjang.

[tex]\boxed{K = 2(p + l)} \\ \\ [/tex]

Formula for perimeter and area of a rectangle

[tex]\boxed{P_{\text{rectangle}} = 2(l + w)} \\ \\\boxed{A_{\text{rectangle}} = l \times w} \\ \\ P \: \: \text{is perimeter of a rectangle} \\ \\ A \: \: \text{is area of a rectangle} \\ \\ l \: \: \text{is length of a rectangle} \\ \\ w \: \: \text{is width of a rectangle} \\ \\ [/tex]

Diketahui :

The perimeter of a rectangle is 84 cm. The length of a rectangle is 5 cm more than 3 times

the width.

Ditanya :

a. The mathematical model

b. Length and width

c. The area of a rectangle

Jawaban :

a. The mathematical model

[tex]P = 84 \\ \\ l = 3w + 5 \\ \\ [/tex]

b. Length and width

[tex]P = 2(l + w) \\ \\ P = 2(3w + 5 + w) \\ \\ P = 2(4w + 5) \\ \\ P = 8w + 10 \\ \\ 84 = 8w + 10 \\ \\ 8w = 84 - 10 \\ \\ 8w = 74 \\ \\ w = \frac{74}{8} \\ \\ \boxed{w = 9.25 \: cm} \\ \\ \\ l = 3w + 5 \\ \\ l = 3(9.25) + 5 \\ \\ l = 27.75 + 5 \\ \\ \boxed{l = 32.75 \: cm} \\ \\ [/tex]

c. The area of a rectangle

[tex]A = l \times w \\ \\ A = 32.75 \times 9.25 \\ \\ \boxed{A = 302.9375 \: {cm}^{2}} \\ \\ [/tex]

Kesimpulan :

a. The mathematical model

[tex]P = 84 \\ \\ l = 3w + 5 \\ \\ [/tex]

b. Length and width

[tex] \boxed{w = 9.25 \: cm} \\ \\ \boxed{l = 32.75 \: cm} \\ \\ [/tex]

c. The area of a rectangle

[tex]\boxed{A = 302.9375 \: {cm}^{2}} \\ \\ [/tex]

14. The width of a rectangle whose the perimeter is 20 cm and the length is 6 cm is … *​


RECTANGLE

The width of a rectangle whose perimeter is 20 cm and the length is 6 cm is [tex]4cm[/tex].

---------------

EXPLANATION

A rectangle is a two-dimensional figure that has four sides in which the opposite sides are parallel and congruent.

The two pairs of parallel sides are called lengths and widths respectfully.

The perimeter of a rectangle is the sum of its sides or you can use the formula below to find it

[tex]Perimeter = 2\times length + 2\times width[/tex]

----------------

STEP BY STEP SOLUTION

Given:

[tex]Perimeter~of~a~rectangle~(P) = 20cm\\

Length~of~the~rectangle~(l)=4cm[/tex]

Find:

[tex]Width~of~the~rectangle~(w)[/tex]

Solution:

1. Use the formula of the perimeter

[tex]P = 2\times l + 2\times w[/tex]

2. Substitute the known values

[tex]20cm = 2\times 6cm + 2\times w\\

20cm=12cm + 2\times w\\

2\times w=20cm-12cm\\

2\times w=8cm\\

w=8cm \div 2\\

w=4cm[/tex]

Therefore, the width of the rectangle is 4 cm.
