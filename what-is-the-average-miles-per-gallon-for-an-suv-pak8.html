10. The average age of the members in a family of five is 28. The average age of the three children is 14. What is the average age of the two adults?


Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Suppose :

x = total age of all family members

y = total age of all children

z = total age of all adults

x/5 = 28

x = 28 (5)

x = 140

y/3 = 14

y = 14 (3)

y = 42

x = y + z

140 = 42 + z

z = 140 - 42

z = 98

The average age of the two adults = 98/2 = 49 years.

15. The average mass of 9 sheep is 35 kg. The average mass of 9 sheep and 1cow is 64 kg. What is the mass of the cow?


Jawaban:

The mass of the cow is 325 kg

Penjelasan:

To be Known :

9 sheep = 35 kg

9 sheep + 1 cow = 64 kg

To be Asked :

mass of the cow = . . . . ?

Solving :

as 9 sheep + 1 cow is 64 kg, so that the total average mass of those 10 animals = 64 kg

total average = [tex]\frac {(9 × 35) + (cow)}{10}[/tex] = 64

(315) + (cow) = 64 × 10

315 + cow = 640

cow = 640 - 315

cow = 325 kg
