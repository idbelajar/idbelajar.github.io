5. A firm produces 400 units of output at a total cost of $1,200. If total variable costs are $1,000 : a. average fixed cost is 50 cents. b. average total cost is 50 cents c. average variable cost is $2 d. average total cost is $2.50 !


d.

Explanation:

Here, it is given that the quantity produced is 400 units.

The total fixed costs is $1200.Out of which, total fixed costs is $200.

So, the total variable cost is $(1200-200)=$1000.

Average fixed costs

= TFC/Q

= 200/400

0,5

average variable cost

=TVC/Q

=$1000/400

=$2.5

total cost

= TC/Q

= $1200/400

=$3

So, the average total cost is $3, the average fixed cost is $0.5 and the average variable cost is $2.5.

12. The average age of the members in a family of five is 28. The average age of the three children is 14. What is the average age of the two adults?


Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Suppose :

x = total age of all family members

y = total age of all children

z = total age of all adults

x/5 = 28

x = 28 (5)

x = 140

y/3 = 14

y = 14 (3)

y = 42

x = y + z

140 = 42 + z

z = 140 - 42

z = 98

The average age of the two adults = 98/2 = 49 years.

15. The average average height of a good is 142 cm theheightis 138 cm. what is the height of the other girls?​


Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Diketahui :

Rata-rata tinggi perempuan = 142 cm

x₁ = tinggi perempuan pertama = 138 cm

Ditanya : x₂

Jawab :

Rata-rata

=> (x₁ + x₂ ) / 2 = 142

=> (138 + x₂ ) = 2 * 142 = 284

=> x₂ = 284 - 138 = 146 cm

Tinggi perempuan kedua = 146 cm
