PART : PART OF SPEECH - SUBJECT-VERB AGREEMENT -CONCORD

___________________

ANSWER :

The two main kinds of magnets are permanent magnets and electromagnets

NOTE/ DISCUSSION  :

The keywords of the sentence above are the indefinite pronoun applied on the front “the two main” and the use of linking verb “are” because they represent the plural context, variety of magnets { more than one kind } . We need to apply the subject –verb agreement , the form of subject  must be similar with its verb . Due to its plural verb {are} , the form of subject should be in the plural form.  

Keep in mind that we must put the preposition “of” after the word “kind” to differentiate the things. We use “kind of magnets”, if the sentence context is in the singular form . Meanwhile, we use “kinds of magnets”, if the sentence context is the plural form. Hence, we must pay attention to  both the form of verb  and its subject in the sentence to link the subject-verb agreement  rule.  

The option A is incorrect because the context of the sentence discusses about the classification or variety of magnets, so it doesn’t appropriate grammatically.

The option D is incorrect because the word “kind” , it should be followed by the proposition “of” to classify the things that we are talking about.

The option B is incorrect because it doesn’t represent grammatical structure. The noun phrase “the two main “ which is preceded before the word “kind of magnet” and also the use of auxiliary verb “are “ represent the plural statement , so the context should be in the similar form.

EXPLANATION: SUBJECT-VERB AGREEMENT

The rule of Subject verb agreement states  that the subject of the sentence and the verb of the sentence must be in agreement in number. It means that, the form of subject in the sentence depends on its form of the verb. If we use the form of singular subject in the sentence, we must imply the form of the singular also.

Example of this concept :

1. The cat is chasing a mouse  

Analysis :

In this case, the subject of the sentence is “the cat” and it is applied in the singular form. So, the verb which follows its subject must use the singular form, adjust on its subject form. The verb “is chasing “ represents the singular form.  

2. The cats are chasing their mouses  

Analysis :

In this case, the subject of the sentence is “The cats” and it is implied in the plural form. The verb which follows its subject must be the plural form, agree with its subject form. The verb “are chasing” represents the plural form.

Cast iron is a group of iron-carbon alloys with a carbon content more than 2%.[1] Its usefulness derives from its relatively low melting temperature. The alloy constituents affect its colour when fractured: white cast iron has carbide impurities which allow cracks to pass straight through, grey cast iron has graphite flakes which deflect a passing crack and initiate countless new cracks as the material breaks, and ductile cast iron has spherical graphite "nodules" which stop the crack from further progressing.

Various examples of cast iron

Carbon (C) ranging from 1.8 to 4 wt%, and silicon (Si) 1–3 wt%, are the main alloying elements of cast iron. Iron alloys with lower carbon content are known as steel.

Cast iron tends to be brittle, except for malleable cast irons. With its relatively low melting point, good fluidity, castability, excellent machinability, resistance to deformation and wear resistance, cast irons have become an engineering material with a wide range of applications and are used in pipes, machines and automotive industry parts, such as cylinder heads, cylinder blocks and gearbox cases.
