4. Tono measures the depth of a pool using a stick. One - quarter of a stick, a length of 24 cm is above the water when the end touches the bottom. How long istthe stick and how deep is the water?​


Solution :

the stick length is 96 cm and the water deep is 72 cm.

Steps and Explanation :

one-quater (¼) of a stick which is 24 cm is above the water when end touches the bottom of the pool.

that means ¼ of stick length is 24 cm. if x is stick length, then :

[tex] \frac{1}{4} x = 24 \\ x = 24 \times 4 \\ x = 96[/tex]

the length of the stick is 96 cm.

Which means, the length of the stick under water is the depth of pool. so, the depth of pool :

[tex](1 - \frac{1}{4} ) \times 96 \\ = \frac{3}{4} \times 96 \\ = 72[/tex]

then the depth of pool is 72 cm.

good luck.

7. A Rectangular Swimming Pool Is 25 Meters Long 6 Meters Wide. What Is The Area Of The Swimming Pool?


Jawaban:

150 m²

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Kolam renang persegi panjang dengan panjang 25 Meter, lebar 6 Meter. Berapa luas kolam renang tersebut ?

Panjang = 25 meter

Lebar = 6 meter

Ditanya = Luas

Luas = panjang dikali lebar

= p × l

= 25 m × 6 m

Luas = 150 m²

Maka, luas kolam renang persegi panjang tersebut adalah 150 m².

So, the area of the rectangular swimming pool is 150 m².

15. A private rectangular swimming pool has a length of 20 m and a breadth of 17 m. The swimming pool is surrounded by a walkway of breadth 1,5 m. The area of the walkway is.


﻿What's known:

﻿A rectangular swimming pool with,

﻿length = 20 m

﻿breadth = 17 m

﻿And it's surrounded by a walkway with,

﻿breadth = 1.5 m

﻿﻿The question:

The area of the walkway.

Answer:

The Area of the swimming pool without the walkway or A1 for short.

﻿length = 20 m

﻿breadth = 17 m

﻿Area = length × breadth

﻿A = 20 × 17

﻿A = 340 m² (A1)

﻿The Area of the swimming pool including the walkway or A2 for short. (+1.5 m)

﻿length = 21.5 m

breadth = 18.5 m

﻿Area = length × breadth

﻿A﻿ = 21.5 × 18.5

﻿A = 397.75 m² (A2)

﻿

﻿Area of the walkway,

﻿A2 - A1

397.75 - 340

﻿57.75 m²

﻿So the Area of the walkway is 57.75 m²
