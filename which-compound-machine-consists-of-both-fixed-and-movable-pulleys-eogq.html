2. Write the difference between Simple Sentence and Compound Sentence, and write 2 examples of both!​


Jawaban:

A simple sentence contains one independent clause. A compound sentence contains more than one

A simple sentence contains a subject and a predicate, but a compound sentence contains more than one subject and more than one predicate.

Examples :

Compound Sentence :

- Our car broke down. We came last

- They spoke to him in English, but he responded in Indonesian

Simple Sentence :

- I go to school everyday

- Our car broke down yesterday

10. Write the difference between Simple Sentence and Compound Sentence, and write 2 examples of both!


Jawaban:

Simple sentence --> Subject (do-er, be-er) + Predicate (message about the subject)

contoh :

She smiled. (subject + verb)

She looked pretty. (subject + verb + complement)

compound sentences --> Sentences + comma + coordinator + sentences

contoh:

She smiled, and she laughed. (Two sentences joined with a comma + coordinator.)

The great tragic artist of the world are four, and three of them are Greek

Penjelasan:

A simple sentence has a subject and a verb and sometimes an object or complement. The subject is who or what the sentence is about. The verb and the object or complement together are often called the predicate. A simple sentence has two parts: its subject and its predicate.

A compound sentence has two or more simple sentences joined together, usually with a comma and a coordinating conjunction.
