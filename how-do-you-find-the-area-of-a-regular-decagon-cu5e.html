6. Help me plsA regular hexagon is circumscribed by a circle of radius 7 cm. Find the area shaded. [4]​


Jawab:

hexagon is made of 6 identical equilateral triangles.

Each triangle side is = radius of circle (see attachment)

area of 1 triangle = 1/2 × base × height

                            = 1/2 × 7 × 3.5√3

                            = 12.25√3 cm²

area of hexagon = 6 triagle

                            = 6 × 12.25√3

                            = 73.5√3 cm²

area of circle = πr²

                      = 22/7 × 7²

                      = 154 cm²

shaded area = circle - hexagon

                     = 154 - 73.5√3

                     ≈ 26.7 cm²
