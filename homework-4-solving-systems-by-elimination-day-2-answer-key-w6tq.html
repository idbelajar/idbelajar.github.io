6. 1. Active: I do my homework every day Passive: Homework are done by me every day 2. Active: Jake’s brothers are shooting the ball Passive: The ball is being shot by Jake’s brothers 3. Active: My mother helps my homework every day Passive: My homework is helped by Mother every day From this sentence, explain whether there is an error or not


Jawaban:

1. Salah

2. Benar

3. Benar

PEMBAHASAN 1. Salah Active: I do my homework every dayPassive: Homework are done by me every dayPenjelasan:

Active-Present Tense

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O

➡ I do my homework every day

Subject => IVerb-1 => doObject => My homework Adverb of time => Every day

Passive-Present Tense

(+) O + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By S

➡ My homework are done by me everyday ❌

Object => My homework Be + Verb-3 => are doneBy subject => By meAdverb of time => Every day

Catatan:

Error pada kalimat Passive bagian Be + V3 (are done). karena my homework adalah tunggal maka menggunakan Be (is).

Jadi, seharusnya:

Passive-Present Tense

(+) O + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By S

➡ My homework is done by me everyday ✅

Object => My homework Be + Verb-3 => is doneBy subject => By meAdverb of time => Every day

2. Benar Active: Jake’s brothers are shooting the ballPassive: The ball is being shot by Jake’s brothersPenjelasan:

Active-Present Continuous

(+) S + is/am/are + V-ing + O

➡ Jake’s brothers are shooting the ball

Subject => Jaka’s brothers ToBe => areVerb-ing => shooting Object => the ball

Passive-Present Continuous

(+) O + is/am/are + Being + V3 + By S

➡ The ball is being shot by Jake’s brothers ✅

Object => The ballTobe => isBeing + V3 => being shotBy Subject => Jake’s brothers

3. Benar

Active: My mother helps my homework every day

Passive: My homework is helped by Mother every day

Penjelasan:

Active-Present Tense

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O

➡ My mother helps my homework every day

Subject => My mother Verb+s => helpsObject => My homework Adverb of time => Every day

Passive-Present Tense

(+) O + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By S

➡ My homework is helped by my mother everyday ✅

Object => My homework Be + Verb-3 => is helpedBy subject => By my mother Adverb of time => Every day

3. The food will be paid by Husen. (present future tense)

7. The homework will have been finished by me before afternoon. (present future perfect tense)

8. The test was done by us yesterday. (simple past tense)

9. Korean drama was being watched by Maria. (past continuous tense)
