2. 1. what is the name of this text?2. what part of the generic structure?


1. Narative text
-> Narative text adalah teks yang menceritakan tentang khayalan. seperti: putri duyung, cinderella, rabbit and crocodile. Termasuk teks fabel yang dimana tokoh nya adalah para binatang dan bertingkah laku seperti manusia (bisa bicara)

2. Orientation, complication, resolution
-> Orientation: berisi pengenalan/pembuka suatu cerita atau berisi tentang penyebab masalah. (Di soal, ada di paragraf 1)
Complication: berisi masalah / konflik yang terjadi dalam suatu cerita. (Ada di paragraf 2 dan 3)
Resolution: berisi tentang selesainya masalah di cerita tersebut. (ada di paragraf 4)-Narrative text is a story with complication or problematic events and it tries to find the resolutions to solve the problems. An important part of narrative text is the narrative mode, the set of methods used to communicate the narrative through a process narration.


-Orientation : It is about the opening paragraph where the characters of the story are introduced.
# Complication : Where the problems in the story developed.
# Resolution : Where the problems in the story is solved.
# Coda / reorientation (optional) – lesson from the story

Questions based on the text!

1. What kind of hobbies are found in the text?

(Hobi macam apakah yang ditemukan dalam teks?)

ANSWER: Stamp collecting. (Koleksi perangko)

Jawaban dapat dilihat pada isi teks email. "I heard from Alex that you are interested in stamp collecting."

2. Please mention some pronoun found in the text!

(Tolong sebutkan beberapa kata ganti yang ditemukan dalam teks!)

ANSWER: you, my, your, I, them.

Jawaban tersebut ada pada kalimat-kalimat berikut.

In case you are wondering, ---> you refer to George.

... my name is Verawati. ---> my merupakan kata ganti milik yang merujuk ke Verawati

I got your e-mail address from ... ---> your merupakan kata ganti milik yang merujuk ke George.

I heard from Alex ... ---> I refer to Verawati.

I just started collecting them ... ---> them refer to stamps.

3. Who sent the email? What is the email address?

(Siapa yang mengirim email? Apa alamat email-nya?)

ANSWER: Verawati. verawati@yahoo.com.

Jawaban dapat dilihat pada baris akhir e-mail, dan alamat pengirim terdapat di bagian atas setelah kata 'From:".

4. Who is the receiver? What is the email address?

(Siapa penerimanya? Apa alamat email-nya?)

ANSWER: Goerge. george.hamilton@hotmail.com.

Jawaban  dapat ditemukan di bagian awal e-mail, greeting. 'Hi George,'. Alamat e-mail dapat dilihat di bagian atas setelah kata 'To:"

5. Why does the sender send the email?

(Mengapa pengirim mengirim email?)

ANSWER: She wants to know Goerge's experience in collecting stamps. It's about how to store and obtain them. (Dia ingin tahu pengalaman George dalam mengoleksi perangko. Ini tentang bagaimana menyimpan dan memperoleh perangko tersebut)

Jawaban dapat diperoleh di bagian akhir isi e-mail, bagian kalimat bercetak tebal.
