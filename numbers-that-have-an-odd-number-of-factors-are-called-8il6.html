14. The sum of 4 cunsencentive and odd number is 64. Find the largest of 4 numbers​


Let, the middle number in the sequence be X.

So, the sequence of five consecutive odd numbers will be,

(X-2),(X-1),X,(X+1),(X+2)

Now, the sum of 64 consecutive odd numbers is 64

So,

(X-2)+(X-1)+X+(X+1)+(X+2)= 64

4X = 64

X = 64/4

X = 16

Find the largest number

= X+2

= 16+2

= 18

So, the largest number in the sequence will be 18.
