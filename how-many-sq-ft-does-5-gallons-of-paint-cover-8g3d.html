Ingredients

200 ml paint = 40g paint powder and 120 ml water.

If one portion of ingredients equals to 200 ml paint, then the total portion needed:

20.000 ml / 200 ml = 100 portions

The total of paint powder needed

100 × 40g = 4000g = 4kg paint powder
