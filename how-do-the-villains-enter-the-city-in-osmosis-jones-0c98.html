6. Mr. Jones will fly from city A to city B.the distance is 45000km/h. The planetravels at 500km/h. How long will theflight take?​


Jawab:

90 h

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah

s = 45000 km

v = 500 km/h

t = s/v

t = 45000 / 500

t = 90 h

the flight will take 90 hour to fly from citu A to city B

Given:

Velocity = 500 km/h

Distance = 45.000 km

Asked:

How long will the flight take ?

Answer:

Time = Distance/Velocity

Time = 45.000/500

Time = 90 hours

So, the flight Mr. Jones took will take 90 hours straight to arrive in city B.
