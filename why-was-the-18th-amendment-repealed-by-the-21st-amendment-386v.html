Pembahasan

Rumus dasar kalimat pasif adalah be + V₃

1. Simple present tense atau Simple past

am / is / are / was / were + V₃ (past participle)

In 2000, my parents bought this motorcycle

passive voice:

In 2000, this motorcycle was bought by my parents

2. Present continuous atau Past future

am / is / are / was / were + being + V₃ (past participle)

The committee is considering several new proposals

passive voice:

Several new proposals are being considered by the committee

3. Present perfect atau past perfect

has / have + had + been + V₃ (past participle)

The company has ordered some new equipment

passive voice:

Some new equipment has been ordered by the company

4. Modals

Modal + be + V₃ (past participle)

you should sign the contract today  

passive voice:

The contract should be signed today

5. Modal + perfect

Modal + have been + V₃ (past participle)

Somebody should have called the police this morning

passive voice:
