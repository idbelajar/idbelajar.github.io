2. please your explain in here what ia the population growth


[ans]

In human geography, population growth is defined as an increase in the number of individual humans in a given geographical area, or administrative region such as cities or provinces.

To determine whether the population grew or decayed, the following equation is used:

[tex]\boxed{\text{population growth}=(\text{natality}+\text{immigration})-(\text{mortality}+\text{emigration})}[/tex]

The four variables above are used to determine the growth or decay of human population in a given area: natality and immigration increases the population; mortality and emigration decreases the population.
