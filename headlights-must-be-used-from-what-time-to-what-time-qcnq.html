14. Asking and Telling about Time The sentences "What time is it?", "What time is it, Kinar?" and "WI the club?" are used to ask about time. Meanwhile, the sentences "It is a qua o'clock." and "At a quarter to four." are used to tell about time. To tell about time, we can use preposition 'at'. The following are expressions to ask and tell about time. Asking about Time Telling about Time ● What time is it?/What is the time? What time do you usually go to school? When do you do your homework? What time does Arka have dinner? ● It is 1 p.m. I usually go to school at 6 o'clock. At 4 o'clock. He has dinner at half past six.
