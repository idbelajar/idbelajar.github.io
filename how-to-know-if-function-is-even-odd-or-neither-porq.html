11. Known to a number of ABBCA. If A prime number is even, B the two squared numbers, and C the first composite number is odd, then ABBCA divided by 4 is ...


ABBCA divided by 4 is 6.123

ABBCA is described separately as a characteristic of the number it represents and produces a number that can be operated.

Prime numbers are natural numbers greater than number 1, where the dividing factor is 1 and the number itself.

Quadratic number (squared number) is a number obtained from the results of multiple multiplication.

Composite numbers are more than 1 natural numbers which are not prime numbers. Composite numbers can be expressed as factorization of integers, or the result of multiplying two or more prime numbers.

DISCUSSION :

Look at the question above.

Known to a number of ABBCA.

• A is an even prime number.

The only even prime number is 2.

• B are two squared numbers.

Two squared numbers which are contained only ones (no tens), are 4 and 9. So, I choose 4.

• and C is the first odd composite number.

The first odd composite number is 9.

So, ABBCA is 24.492.

If 24.992 is divided by 4, it will be :

24.992 ÷ 4 = 6.123
