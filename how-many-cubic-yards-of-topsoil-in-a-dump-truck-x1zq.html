1. A runner runs 5 yards every 10 minutes. How many yards can he run in 30 minutes?


Jawaban:

15 yards

Penjelasan:

5 yard = 10 minutes

... yard = 30 minutes

[tex]30 \div 10 = 5 \div ...[/tex]

[tex]3 = 5 \div ....[/tex]

[tex]3 \times 5 = ....[/tex]

[tex] = 15[/tex]

[tex] \frac{5 \: yards}{10 \: mins} = \frac{x}{30 \: mins} \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: x = \frac{5 \times 30}{10} \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: x = \frac{150}{10} \\ \: \: \: x = 15[/tex]

So, the runner can run 15 yards in 30 minutes.

4. a truck travels 0.72 km on 0.01 litre of fuel how many kilomatetres will it travel on 1 litre of fuel?


Soal ini dapat dikerjakan dengan cara perbandingan senilai

0,72 km → 0,01 L

x km → 1 L

.

[tex] \frac{0,72}{x} [/tex] = [tex] \frac{0,01}{1} [/tex]

0,72 = x . 0,01

[tex] \frac{0,72}{0,01} [/tex] = x

72 = x

Maka, jarak yg dapat ditempuh dengan 1 L bensin adalah 72 km

11. A cubic container is filled with 1000cm³ of water. If 1197cm³ of the water is added, find the height, in cm, of water in the container.​


Short answer:

13 cm

Explanations:

Cubic container = 1.000 cm³ + 1.197 cm³

Cubic container = 2.197 cm³

Cubic container = side x side x side

2.197 cm³ = side³

side = [tex]\sqrt[3]{2.197}[/tex] cm

side = 13 cm

So, the height of water in the container is 13 cm
