Example: The velocity of a particle moving along the x axis varies in time according to the expression vx = (40 - 5t2) m / s, where t is in seconds. a. Find the average acceleration in the time interval t = 0 to t = 2.0 s b. Determine the acceleration at t = 2.0 s.


Jawaban:

a. - 10 m/s²

b. - 20 m/s²

Penjelasan:

Vx = (40 - 5t²)

a.

saat t₁ = 0 --> Vx₁ = 40 m/s

saat t₂ = 2 --> Vx₂ = 40 - 5. 4 = 20 m/s

Average acceleration

ar = ΔV/Δt = ( Vx₂ - Vx₁ ) / ( t₂ - t₁ )

ar = ( 20 - 40 )/( 2 - 0 )

ar = - 10 m/s²  ( perlambatan )

b.

acceleration at t = 2.0 s.

a = dV/dt = - 10. t

a = - 10. 2 = - 20 m/s²

12. Mr Raj walked 420 m at a speed of 105 m/min from the bus interchange to the swimming complex. He then took 3 min to walk from the swimming complex to his home. Mr Raj walked a total distance of 896 m. (a) Find the total time taken for the whole journey. (b) Find Mr Raj's average speed for the whole journey.​


Jawaban:

a. 7 minutes

b. 128 m/min

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

given that:

total distance = 896 m

distance from bus int. to swim comp. = 420 m

speed from bus int. to swim comp. = 105 m/min = 1.75 m/s

time from swim comp. to home = 3 minutes

question:

a. total time

b. average speed

answer:

a. total time

total time = (time from bus int. to swim comp.) + (time from swim comp. to home)

= (420 m ÷ 1.75 m/s) + 3 minutes

= 240 s + 180 s

= 420 seconds

= 7 minutes

b. average speed

average speed = total distance / total time

= 896 m/7 minutes

= 128 m/minutes

= 2.13 m/s
