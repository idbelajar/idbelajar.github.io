2. The book is on the table. the interrogative form (?) of this sentence is...a. does The book is on the table?b. is The book is on the table?c. are The book is on the table?d. do The book is on the table?


Jawaban:

Bentuk interrogative dari "the book is on the table" adalah :

Is the book on the table?

Jawabannya : B

Penjelasan:

Karena kalimat positive nya bentuk present nominal, dimana rumus nya :

(+) S + To be (am/are/is) + Complement

(-) S + To be (am/are/is) + not + complement

(?) To be (am/are/is) + S + complement

Dan struktur dari kalimat "The book is on the table" :

The book => Subject

Is => To be

On the table => Adverb of place

================================
