10. The energy stored in a spring is directly proportional to the square off its extension. When ada spring is stretched by d cm, the energy stired in it is E joules (E). If the extension of the springs decreased by 25%, find the percentage decrease in the energy stored


Because of the decrease of the extension of the springs, then
[tex]d_{2} = d_{1}(1 - 0.25) = 0.75 \: d_{1}[/tex]

The ratio between current energy and its previous is
[tex] \frac{E_{2} }{E_{1} } = {( \frac{ d_{2} }{ d_{1} }) }^{2} = {0.75}^{2} = 0.5625 \\ E_{2} = 0.5625 \: E_{1} = (1 - 0.4375)E_{1} [/tex]

So, the energy stored in the spring decreased by 43.75%.

13. The energy stored in a spring is directly proportional to


Jawab:

The constant spring (K)

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The formula of potential (stored) energy in a spring (PE) can be defined as

PE = ½ kx²

Thus, the PE in a spring is directly proportional to the constant spring (k) and proportional to the square of extended distance in the spring.
