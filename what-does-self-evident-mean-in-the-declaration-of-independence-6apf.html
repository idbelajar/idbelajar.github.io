5. what does it mean independence day for you?


Definition of Independence

The word is derived from the word independence independence. In Big Indonesian Dictionary we find that the meaning of the word independent is: 1) freedom from slavery, colonialism, etc.., Stand-alone; 2) are not exposed to or separated from the claims; 3) not bound, does not depend on a particular person or party. While independence is a state (p) stand-alone (free, loose, no longer occupied); freedom. Based on the above understanding I try to apply the word independent in my own self. Have I free? When viewed from the first sense (freedom from slavery, colonization, stand-alone), as a woman, I feel, at times, still occupied by worldly desires such as clothing and accessories complement. I still shackled my passion for thinking about more stuff than I need. I have not been free from colonial lust of my own, I mean not free! Judging from the second sense (not exposed to or separated from the claims), I can not escape from the demands of a lifetime obligation, either as a child, a wife, mother, and community members. Again, I am not free! Viewed from any third sense (not bound, not dependent on a particular party), I am not a free man. As a child, I still need opinions, suggestions, and support from my parents. As a wife, I am in desperate need of attention, affection, and support her husband. As a mother, I also need the love and support my children. As a member of society, I also need the friendship, friendship with other community members. I still need certain people in my life. If so, where it is freedom to me, my personal freedom?

Understanding Self / Private

Understanding yourself in the Big Indonesian Dictionary is: 1) a person (apart from the others), the body; 2) not with the others; 3) complement verb which states that the sufferer or the goal is its own entity. While personal understanding is: 1) the human as an individual (man or yourself); 2) the state of human beings as individuals, the overall properties of the nature of man. If connected with the word independence, the sense of freedom itself can be interpreted as the freedom of self - with human nature as a whole - from dependence on other parties. To determine whether or not our dependence on others, we have to do first is to know the advantages and disadvantages diri.Dengan other words, we should recognize ourselves first. How do we identify ourselves? How to identify the following self I know from personal development course that never existed in the city, which states that self-knowledge is an early stage of the development process itself. The introduction of self begins with record ourselves honestly, negative positive traits we feel or have. Then we must seek feedback from someone who we can trust (parents, relatives, relatives, friends, teachers, etc.) to get confirmation also honest about our properties. Furthermore, we must accept the fact gracefully results confirm that, and we use as a starting point to maximize the positive qualities and minimize our negative nature. From the description on how to recognize ourselves, we can see that to recognize ourselves we still need confirmation from others. In other words, we still Bergan tung to the other party. We are not free! Again we ask, where is the freedom of our self?

Jawaban:

1. different artinya adalah berbeda
Contoh kalimat:
Your house is different to mines.

2. natural artinya adalah alami
Contoh kalimat:
Your look is really natural.

3. cume artinya adalah manis
Contoh kalimat:
That sugar is really cume.

4. live artinya adalah tinggal (tinggal di rumah)
Contoh kalimat:

I live in Bekasi.

5. warm artinya adalah hangat
Contoh kalimat:
That tea is warm.

6. tiny artinya adalah sangat kecil  
Contoh kalimat:
The ant is tiny.

7. hole artinya adalah lubang
Contoh kalimat:
I always see a small hole in my bag.

8. inside artinya adalah di dalam (suatu gedung / rumah / bangunan)
Contoh kalimat:
My sister is always inside her room doing homework.

9. squeeze artinya adalah memencet (suatu benda) / meremas

Contoh kalimat:
I'm going to squeeze an orange to make juice.

10. mop up artinya adalah membersihkan cairan tumpah
Contoh kalimat:
My mom is mopping up all the spilled water.
