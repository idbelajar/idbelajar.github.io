= a + b + c

= 5 + 5 + 8

= 18

s = 1/2 x 18

s = 9

Luas

= √(s(s - a)(s - b)(s - c))

= √(9(9 - 5)(9 - 5)(9 - 8))

= √(9 x 4 x 4 x 1)

= √144

= 12 satuan luas
