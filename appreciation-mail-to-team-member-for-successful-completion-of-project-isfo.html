7. contoh asking for attention checking for understanding asking for and giving opinions showing appreciation appreciation to others speaker speaker​


Sebelum membuat dialog mengenai asking for attention, checking understanding, appreciation and compliment, and asking for and giving opinion, kita sebaikya mengetahui kalimat seperti apa yang dapat digunakan untuk mengekspresikannya.

1. Expression of asking for attention

- Attention, please!

- Stop talking please!

- Everybody may i have your attention, please?

- Please, look at me

2. Expression of checking someone's understanding

- Do you get it?

- Do you understand what i'm saying?

- Are you with me?

- Do you get what i mean?

3. Expression of giving compliment/ appreciation

- What a ...

- How ...

- That's a ....

- You're so ...

4. Expression of Asking and giving opinion

- What you do think about ... ?

- What do you think of ... ?

- Do you think ... ?

- I think ...

- I feel ...

- In my opinion, ...

- For me, ...

Berikut contoh dialognya

Student A: Attention, please! (asking for attention)

Student B: What's up, A?

Student A: I believe everyone has heard about the earthquake which happened in Lombok, right?

Other students: Yes.

Student A: So, i have an idea. What if our class collects money, groceries, or used clothes to be donated to them. What do you think about it, guys? (Asking opinion)

Student C: Wow, What a great idea! (giving apprecation/ compliment)

Student D: Yeah, I agree with you. I think we should help our friends who are in Lombok. (giving opinion)

Student E: But how do we send our donation?

Student A: My cousin will be a volunteer in Lombok, He will go to Lombok On Sunday. I can ask him to bring our donation.

Student E: Great. We have to collect our donation as soon as possible.

Student A: Yes, You're right. The due date is on Saturday. Do you get it, guys? (checking understanding)

Other students: Yes!

Mudah-mudahan jawaban diatas dapat membantu menjawab pertanyaanmu.
