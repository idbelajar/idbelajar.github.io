8. Hi, how to find the surface area of a cube? Please help me cuz I can't find it in Google.


AnswerFormula

Surface area of a cube = 6 × a²

Example

Find the surface area of a cube with a side of length 5 cm!

Solution:

Surface area of cube = 6 × a²

                                   = 6 × 5²

                                   = 6 × 25

                                   = 150

Therefore, the surface area of that cube is 150 cm²

14. Rectangle A overlaps Square B as shown. The ratio of the area of Rectangle A to the shaded area to the area of Square B is 6:1:4 The area of Rectangle A is 36 cm2. Find the area of the whole figure.


Ratio of the area of Rectangle A =
6 units

6 units = 36 cm2
1 unit = 36 ÷ 6 = 6 cm2

Ratio of total area = 6 + 1 + 4 = 11 units

11 units = 11 x 6 = 66 cm2

The area of the whole figure is 66 cm2.
