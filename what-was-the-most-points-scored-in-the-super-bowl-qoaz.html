1. In a game of darts, Benny scored 6 points more than the total number of points which Caleb and David scored. Caleb scored 2 points more than David. If the total score of the 3 boys was 42, how many points did Benny score?.


Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Answer:

Score of Benny = 24

Step-by-step explanation:

let score of Benny be x

let score of Caleb be y

let score of David be z

x = 6 + y + z (1)

y = 2 + z (2)

x + y + z = 42 (3)

6 + y + z + y + z = 42 from (1)

2(y + z) = 36

y + z = 36/2

y + z = 18

2 + z + z = 18 From (2)

2z = 18 - 2

2z = 16

z = 16/2

z = 8

On substituting value of z in (2)

y = 2 + 8

y = 10

On substituting value of y and z in (1)

x = 6 + 10 + 8

x = 24

Score of Benny = x = 24

x = y + z + 6...(1)

Total score of them was 42 ( Jumlah skor ketiganya adalah 42)

Ditanya:

skor Benny? (x)

Jawab:

Eliminasi persamaan 1 dan persamaan 3

x = y + z + 6

x - y - z = 6..(1)

x + y + z = 42...(3)

-------------------------- -

-2y -2z = -36

2y + 2z = 36

y + z = 18...(4)

Eliminasi persamaan 2 dan persamaan 4

y = z + 2

y - z = 2 ...(2)

y + z = 18...(4)

-------------------- -

-2z = -16

z = 8

Subtitusi nilai z ke persamaan 2

y = z + 2

y = 10

Subtitusi nilai y dan z ke persamaan 1

x = y + z + 6

x = 10 + 8 + 6

x = 24

Maka skor Benny adalah 24
