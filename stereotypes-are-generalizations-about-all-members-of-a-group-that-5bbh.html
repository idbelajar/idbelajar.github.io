9. A swimming club has 360 members. 35% of the members are boys, 40% are girls and the rest are adults. How many members are adults?


Jawaban:

90 members

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

360 members

boys = 35% of 360

boys = 35/100 of 360

boys = 126 members

girls = 40% of 360

girls = 40/100 of 360

girls = 4/10 of 360

girls = 144 members

adults = total members - boys - girls

adults = 360 - 126 - 144

adults = 90 members

Jawaban:
