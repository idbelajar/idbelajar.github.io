7. How many comparison have you learned? Mention and explain it!​


Jawaban:

Degrees of Comparison

=> Degrees of Comparison adalah pola kalimat dalam bahasa inggris untuk membandingakan suatu objek. Comparison yang telah saya pelajari memiliki 3 bagian yaitu :

Positive Degree untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang tidak memiliki perbandingan atau sama. kata sinyalnya 'as'. contoh: My sister is as clever as me.

Comparative Degree untuk menyatakan perbandingan antara 2 objek. dengan kata sinyal 'than'. contoh: My sister is cleverer than me.

Superlative Degree untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang paling, dan tidak ada yang membandingi. contoh: My sister is cleverest person in my family.

8. degree of comparison is divided into how many parts ? and explain it one by one !


Degree of comparison is divided into 3 parts :
1. Positive degree
2. Comparative degree
3. Superlative degree

Explanation :
1. Positive degree
To use when we speak about only one person or thing.
Example :
• He is a smart student
• It is a beautiful flower

2. Comparative degree
To use when we compare two things or two person with each other.

✓ For 1 syllable & 2 syllable that end with y letter we use word+er
Example :
• She is taller than me
• He feel luckier than tomorrow

✓ For 2 syllables or more we use more + word
Example :
• This shirt is more expensive than that one

✓ There's some of the words that have irregular comparative forms, such as :
• good = better
• bad = worse
• far = further
• little = less

✓ For positive form that 2 things or 2 persons are the same we use as...as.
Example :
• My house is as big as your house

3. Superlative degree
To use when we compare more than two things or persons with one another.

✓ For 1 syllable & 2 syllable that end with y letter we use the + word+est
Example :
• She is the luckiest person in my family
• He is the tallest boy in the class

✓ For 2 syllables or more we use the + most + word
Example :
• He is the most handsome model in that agency.

✓ There's some of the words that have irregular comparative forms, such as :
• good = better = best
• bad = worse = worst
• far = further = furthest
• little = less = least

10. how to cook lemper? explain it​


Jawaban:

How to cook chicken lemper

Ingredients:

500 gram white glutinous rice2 cups water3/4 cup coconut milkA pinch of salt

For filling

200 gm minced chicken breast2 teaspoon coriander 1 teaspoon cumin 1/2 cup coconut milk 1/2 teaspoon belacan (trassi) 2 cloves garlic, chopped 1 onion, chopped 2 kaffir lime leaves Salt and pepper to taste Oil for cooking Squares of banana leaf

Directions

Prep:15min  ›  Cook:40min  ›  Extra time:5min  ›  Ready in:1hr

1. Soak the rice in a bowl of water for an hour.

2.  Prepare filling:

3.  Heat a little oil in saucepan and saute the onions, garlic and kaffir lime leaves. Add the ground spices, chicken and mash the belacan with the other ingredients and stir well. Pour in the coconut milk. Add salt and pepper and cook until the chicken is almost dry. Let cool.

 

4. Drain and rinse the rice. Put the rice in a rice cooker, add water and steam for 15 minutes.Add the coconut milk and a pinch of salt to the rice and fluff it well with a fork. Cover the pot and let it steam for another 10-15 minutes until the rice is cooked.

 

5. Take a large tablespoon of rice and place onto a banana leaf square. Flatten the rice to 1/2 in thickness then put a full teaspoon of filling in the center. Shape the rice into a mound covering the filling. Roll the banana leaf and secure the ends with toothpicks
