1. The average age of the members in a family of five is 28. The average age of the three children is 14. What is the average age of the two adults?


Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Suppose :

x = total age of all family members

y = total age of all children

z = total age of all adults

x/5 = 28

x = 28 (5)

x = 140

y/3 = 14

y = 14 (3)

y = 42

x = y + z

140 = 42 + z

z = 140 - 42

z = 98

The average age of the two adults = 98/2 = 49 years.

4. The density of aluminium is 2.7 kg/m3. If the mass of aluminium is 8.1 grams, what is the volume of the aluminium in m3?​


Jawaban:

[tex]\sf\large{V=0,003\ m³}[/tex]

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The known things are;

[tex]\sf\large{ρ = 2,7\ kg/m³}[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{m = 8,1\ g = 0,0081\ kg}[/tex]

The asked of the volume of aluminium

The answer is;

Use the formula;

[tex]\sf\large{ρ} = \LARGE{\frac{m}{V}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{V} = \LARGE{\frac{m}{ρ}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{V} = \LARGE{\frac{0,0081}{2,7}}[/tex]

[tex]\sf\large{V = 0,003\ m³}[/tex]

So, the volume of the aluminium is [tex]\sf\large0,003\ m³[/tex]

8. what is the function of high- density lipoprotein?Brainly​


Jawaban:

Although high-density lipoproteins (HDL) possess many features that contribute to the association between elevated HDL cholesterol and protection from atherosclerosis, these lipoproteins may be modified in certain individuals and/or circumstances to become proinflammatory. The ability of HDL to inhibit or paradoxically to enhance vascular inflammation, lipid oxidation, plaque growth, and thrombosis reflects changes in specific enzyme and protein components. The anti-inflammatory and proinflammatory functional properties of HDL can now be assessed using cell-based and cell-free assays. Acute or chronic systemic inflammation and the metabolic syndrome appear to render HDL proinflammatory. In contrast, statins and experimental agents such as apolipoprotein A-1 mimetics render HDL more anti-inflammatory. Functional characterization of HDL is a promising method for enhanced assessment of cardiovascular risk and effectiveness of risk reduction.

10. The average mass of 9 sheep is 35 kg. The average mass of 9 sheep and 1cow is 64 kg. What is the mass of the cow?


Jawaban:

The mass of the cow is 325 kg

Penjelasan:

To be Known :

9 sheep = 35 kg

9 sheep + 1 cow = 64 kg

To be Asked :

mass of the cow = . . . . ?

Solving :

as 9 sheep + 1 cow is 64 kg, so that the total average mass of those 10 animals = 64 kg

total average = [tex]\frac {(9 × 35) + (cow)}{10}[/tex] = 64

(315) + (cow) = 64 × 10

315 + cow = 640

cow = 640 - 315

cow = 325 kg

15. the average of seven numbers is 38.The sum of the first six number is 156.what is the seventh number?


Jawab:  110

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The average of seven numbers is 38,

(rata-rata dari ketujuh nomor adalah 38)

38 comes from The Sum of seven numbers : 7 (total amount numbers)

(38 didapat dari jumlah ketujuh nomor dibagi 7 (banyaknya nomor) )

so, The Sum of seven numbers = 38x7 = 266

(maka, total jumlah dari ketujuh nomor adalah = 38x7 = 266)

The sum of the first - six number is 156,

(total jumlah nomor dari 1 sampai 6 adalah 156) => tertera di soal

The Sum of seven numbers = 38x7 = 266,

(Total jumlah keseluruhan nomor adalah 266)

so, The seven number is 266-156 = 110

maka, angka ke 7 adalah 266-156 = 110
