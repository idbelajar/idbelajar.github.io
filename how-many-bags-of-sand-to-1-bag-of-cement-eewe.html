14. A company planned to donate 640 packs of instant noodles, 320 bottles of mineral water and $800 worth of cash to an old folks home. Suppose that the maximum number of packs of instant noodles, bottles of mineral water and cash are to be placed equally in gift bags before the donation. a. How many gift bags are needed? b. List the item in each gift bag.


Jawaban:

a. 160 bags

b.

4 packs of Instant Noodles2 bottles of Mineral Water, and$5 worth of cash

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

First of all you need to find the Highest Common Factor of: 640, 320 and 800

[tex] 640 = {2}^{7} \times 5[/tex]

[tex]320 = {2}^{6} \times 5[/tex]

[tex]800 = {2}^{5} \times {5}^{2} [/tex]

The Highest Common Factor is:

[tex] {2}^{5} \times 5 = 160[/tex]

Answer:

a. Instant Noodles :

[tex]640 \div 160 = 4 \: packs[/tex]

Mineral Water:

[tex]320 \div 160 = 2 \: bottles[/tex]

Cash:

[tex]800 \div 160 = 5 \: dollar[/tex]
