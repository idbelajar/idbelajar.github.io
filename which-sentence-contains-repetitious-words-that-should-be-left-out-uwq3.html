10. WHAT ARE THE WORDS SHOULD BE PARALELLIZED IN ENGLISH SENTENCE? Why the English sentence has the rules to write and speak should be parallelized? What wrong has been mistaken if not parallel in the sentence?


Jawaban:

English has the rules to parallelized sentences both in written and spoken context because it adds more clarity and makes the sentence easier to read. If the sentence is not parallelized (i.e. She likes ice skating and play skateboard.), the sentence will sound off and unclear.

The words should be parallelized according to their grammatical pattern. For instance in the sentence She likes ice skating and play skateboard., if one had used the term ice skating, the word play skateboard should be substituted with skateboarding to make the sentence parallel.
