3. (+) they have lived in the california since 1987 (-) (?)


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS
MATERI : PRESENT PERFECT TENSE
====================
Rumus :
(+) subject + have/has + V3 + object
(-) subject + have/has + not + V3 + object
(?) Have/Has + Subject + V3 + object

CATATAN !!!
* Have di gunakan untuk subject I/you/they/we
* Has di gunakan untuk subject She/he/it.

JAWABAN :
(+) They have lived in the California since 1987.
(-) They have not lived in the California since 1987
(?) Have they lived in the California since 1987?

Present perfect tense adalah kalimat tense yang dipakai untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang sudah dimulai di masa lalu dan masih berlanjut sampai sekarang. Atau sudah selesai di waktu tertentu di masa lalu, tapi efeknya masih terasa sampai sekarang.

Formulasi present perfect tense

S + have/has + past participle (verb 3) + O

Have dipakai untuk subyek I, you, we, they

Has dipakai untuk subyek he, she, it

Contoh:

(+) She has worked in the hospital for three years.

Arti: Dia sudah bekerja di rumah sakit selama tiga tahun.

(-) She hasn't worked in the hospital for three years.

Arti: Dia belum/tidak bekerja di rumah sakit selama tiga tahun.

(?) Has she worked in the hospital for three years?

Arti: Sudahkah dia bekerja di rumah sakit selama  tiga tahun?


Pembahasan

(+) They have lived in California since 1987.

(-) They haven't lived in California since 1987.

(?) Have they lived in California since 1987?
