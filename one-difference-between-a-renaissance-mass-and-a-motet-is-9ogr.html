13. The mass of Bag A is ¾ kg. Bag A is the heaviest. The difference between the masses of Bag A and Bag B is 7/12 kg. Bag B is the lightest.The difference between the masses of Bag B and Bag C is 1/6 kg.What is the mass of Bag C? Express n explanation your answer in its simplest form.​


Jawaban:

1/3 kg

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

berat A=3/4 kg

Berat A- berat B= 7/12 kg

3/4- berat b=7/12

berat b= 3/4-7/12=2/12=1/6 kg

Berat c- berat b= 1/6 kg

berat c -1/6=1/6

berat c= 1/3 kg

Diketahui:

[tex]A = \frac{3}{4} = \frac{9}{12}[/tex]

[tex]A - B = \frac{7}{12}[/tex]

[tex]C - B = \frac{1}{6} = \frac{2}{12}[/tex]

Ditanya: C ?

Jawab:

[tex]A - B = \frac{7}{12}\\ \\ B = \frac{9}{12} - \frac{7}{12} = \frac{2}{12}[/tex]

substitusikan nilai B ke persamaan C-B

[tex]C - B = \frac{2}{12}\\ \\ C = \frac{2}{12} + \frac{2}{12} \\ \\C = \frac{4}{12} = \frac{1}{3}[/tex]

jadi massa dari tas C adalah [tex]\frac{1}{3}[/tex] kg
