1. 1 Bat+1 Ball =$1.10 the bat is 1 dollar more than the ball. how much is the ball cost?


1 Bat + 1 Ball = $1.10
1 Bat = $1 + 1Ball

So,
1 Bat + 1 Ball = $1.10
($1 + 1 Ball) + 1 Ball = $1.10
$1 + 2 Ball = $1.10
2 Ball = $1.10 - $1
2 Ball = $0.10
1 Ball = ($0.10) / 2
1 Ball = $0.05
