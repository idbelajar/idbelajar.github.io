5. Mathew borrowed $24 000 from a bank. He paid 5% interest to the bank in the first year. How much interest did Mathew pay in the first year?​


Given : Principal = $24000 ( P )

Interest Rate = 5% = 0,05 ( R )

time = 1 years ( t )

Ask: Interest ( I ) = ........?

Answer :

Yearly

I = R.P.t

= 24000. 0,05.1

= $1200

So, Mathew pay interest $1200 in the first year
