11. A ball travelling at 25 m/s and has a kinetic energy of 50 J. Calculate the mass of the ball !​


velocity (v) = 25 m/s

KE = 50 J

mass (m) = ....?

KE = ½ × m × v²

50 = ½ × m × 25²

50 = ½ × m × 625

312.5m = 50

m = 50/312.5

m = 4/25
