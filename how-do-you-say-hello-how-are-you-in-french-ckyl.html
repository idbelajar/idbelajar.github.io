5. why do we say "How are you? "​


Jawaban:As far as the grammar part is concerned….

The plural of FIRST Personal Pronoun I …

WE

The singular and plural of SECOND Personal Pronoun …

YOU & YOU

The plural of THIRD Personal Pronoun HE/SHE …

THEY

WE.… YOU.…and THEY are followed by the verb ARE.….. off-shoot of the principal verb BE.

The interrogative sentence will be......

HOW ARE YOU !!!!!

The person speaking does not say this sentence to seek some information.

It is simply a form of courteous greeting !!

Penggunaan

Biasanya "How do you do? " digunakan untuk menanyakan kabar seseorang untuk kepertama kalinya.

Penggunaan ini juga dibalas kalimat yang sama, melainkan melibatkan nama seseorang yang pertama kali menanya.

Contoh 1 :

Atha : "how do you do?"

Kuyang : "how do you do Atha?"

Contoh 2 :

Alfian : how do you do?

Kuyang : how do you do Alfian?
