m = 2 kg
∆T = (52-12) °C= 40 °C
Q = 192 kJ = 192000 J

Dit. c = ?

Jawab;
Q = m.c.∆T
c = Q/(m.∆T)
c = 192000/(2.40)
c = 192000/80 = 2400 J/(kg.°C)
