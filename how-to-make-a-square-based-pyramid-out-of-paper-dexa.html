5. soal how to make a paper boat or hat


How to Make a Paper Boat:

Material:
- HVS/Rectangle Paper

Steps:
1. First, fold the paper in half.
2. Second, fold the right and left corner into a triangle.
3. Next, fold the bottom of the triangle to the top. Do the same for the back until it looks like a hat.
4. Then, open the folds of the triangle into a square.
5. After that, fold the bottom part of the square to the top. Do it again for the back.
6. Next, open the fold into another square.
7. Finally, pull the ends of right and left to form a boat with a triangle in the middle.
8. Voila! Your boat is ready to sail ^_^

15. Paul had a square piece of paper. He cut out a rectangle that was 4 centimeters by 9 centimeters. The area of the left over paper was 108 square centimeters. What was the length of the sides of Paul's original sauare?​


Penjelasan:

Diketahui persegi panjang yg diptoong keluar sebesar 4x9 cm

Sisa kertas 108cm^2

Cari panjang kertas awal

pertama cari luas kertas potongan

=4x9 =36 cm^2

Luas awal = 108 + 36 = 144 cm^2

Dikatakan itu square pas awalnya maka

S^2 = ✓144 = 12

So, the length of the sides of Paul's original square is 12 centimeter
