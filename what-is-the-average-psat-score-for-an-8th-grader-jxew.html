1. 3. The average of Math's score of four students is 6.9 If the teacher add the average with Bertha Math's score, the average become 7.2 What is Bertha Math's score?


Jawab:

8.4

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Nilai Bertha = (7.2 x 5) - (6.9 x 4)

Nilai Bertha = 36 - 27.6 = 8.4

Bertha's Math score is 8.4

11. a boy has an average score of 5.375 for 4 Indonesian language exams. then the boy joins one remedial exam. to reach the mean at least 6.00, what is the minimum score he must get from the remedial exam?


5,375 for 4 exam. It means his total score is
4(5,375) = 21,5

How to make the mean at least 6,00 for 5 exams (4 exams before and a remedial after it) so the total score should be 30,00.
We can know his score for last exam by :
5(6,00) - 4(5,375)
= 30,00 - 21,50
= 8,50

So, he must get at least 8,50 in remidial exam to get the mean 6,00
