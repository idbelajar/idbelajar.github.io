4. A village loses 14% of its goats in a flood and 6% of the remainder die from diseases. Given that the number of goats left is 8084, find the original number of goats in the village.​


Jawaban:

Let G represent (take the place of) the original number of goats in the village.

Write down all the facts you know.

We started with G.

Then 14% died.

Then 6% of what was left died.

Then 8084 were left finally.

"Lost" and "died" suggest these are "take aways" ('-' signs). :^)

The first loss:

0.14G

The remainder after the first loss:

G - 0.14G

The second loss:

0.06 * (G - 0.14G)

G - two losses:

G - 0.14G - 0.06 * (G - 0.14G) = 8084

G - 0.14G - (0.06)(0.86G) = 8084

Factor out the G (so you can divide both sides "cleanly" leaving G on just one side:

G * (1 - 0.14 - 0.0516) = 8084

G * (0.8084) = 8084

Divide both sides by (0.8084) [note: dividing both sides of an EQUATION by the SAME number preserves EQUALITY]:

G = 8084/0.8084 = 10,000

15. When an integer is divided by 10, 15 and 18, it leaves a remainder of 5 respectively. Find the smallest of the number. ​


Sebuah bilangan dibagi 10, 15, dan 18 masing-masing menyisakan 5. Bilangan terkecil dari bilangan bulat tersebut adalah 95.

Pembahasan

Misal bilangan bulat tersebut adalah N.

N = 10k + 5

N = 15m + 5

N = 18p + 5

k, m, p bilangan bulat.

10 = 2 x 5

15 = 3 x 5

18 = 2 x 3²

KPK(10, 15, 18) = 2 x 3² x 5 = 90

artinya kemungkinan N adalah

N - 5 = 90

N = 95
