The word Industrial Revolution is already familiar to us right now, right now, in terms of its economy, Indonesia often associates it with the existence of the Industrial Revolution. What was the Industrial Revolution? The Industrial Revolution itself means a Revolution which means a change quickly and concerns the basis of people's life, and Industry itself is an economic activity that processes raw materials or semi-finished materials into quality goods in their use for the general public. Meanwhile, the Industrial Revolution is a change that occurs rapidly in the implementation of the production process or the way of making or increasing the use value of an item that originally used human labor (traditional) switched to using (modern) machine tools.

The world has now entered the 4th Industrial Revolution (Industry 4.0) and from the journey of the world revolution, of course Indonesia must be ready to face the same progress as other countries, namely by the presence of this 4th Industrial Revolution. One of the most visible advances from various aspects in terms of how often people use it in their daily lives is the internet.

The internet is very important in terms of encouraging an industrial revolution that will run well, without the help of the internet, of course the industrial revolution cannot run right? Mentioning a little about the internet in Indonesia, the internet itself entered Indonesia in the era of the 1990s. At that time, the internet network in Indonesia was better known as the paguyuban network, where the spirit of cooperation and mutual cooperation was very warm among its users. After several years the internet has entered the life of Indonesian people, until now it is estimated that there are approximately 143 million internet users throughout Indonesia, according to the Indonesian Internet Service Providers Association (APJII). In contrast to the atmosphere of the Indonesian internet, in its later development, it felt more commercial and individual in some of its activities, especially internet trade, which is nothing but closely related to the industrial system and the running of the economy in Indonesia.
