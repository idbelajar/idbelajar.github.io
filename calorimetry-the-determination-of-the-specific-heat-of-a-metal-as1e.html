6. The heat required to raise the temperature of 7 kg of water from 10 oC to 80 oC and The specific heat of the water 4200 J/kg oC is


The heat required to raise the temperature of 7 kg of water from 10°C to 80°C and The specific heat of the water 4.200 J/kg°C is 2.058.000 Joule.

Solution:

Known:

m = 7 kg

∆T = 80°C – 10°C = 70°C

c = 4.200 J/kg°C

Asked:

Q = ...?

Answer:

Q = m × c × ∆T

= 7 × 4.200 × 70

= 2.058.000 Joule or 2.058 kJ.
