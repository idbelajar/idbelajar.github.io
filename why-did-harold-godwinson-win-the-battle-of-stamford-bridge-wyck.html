The passage is about the Battle of Surabaya, a historical battle in Indonesia that took place in 1945.The battle took place on November 10th, 1945.The battle happened in Surabaya, which is located in East Java, Indonesia.The battle was caused by a misunderstanding between the British troops in Jakarta and those in Surabaya, where the British troops dropped leaflets telling the Indonesians to surrender their weapons, despite an agreement made between the British and the Governor of East Java to not ask for the weapons to be surrendered.The passage does not mention any comparison of the Indonesian military power to that of the British army at that time.The Indonesians were provoked to face the British army military aggression as they felt betrayed after the British troops dropped leaflets telling them to surrender their weapons despite an agreement made between the British and the Governor of East Java not to ask for the weapons to be surrendered.The passage does not mention if the Indonesians lost or won the battle, but it states that the city was conquered by the British troops within three days, and that many Indonesians died in the battle.The battle influenced the national revolution at that time by provoking Indonesian and international mass to rally for the country's independence.Bung Tomo is the prominent figure in the battle and he played a very important role in the Battle of Surabaya.November 10th is used as a momentum to commemorate the hero's contribution because it is the anniversary of the Battle of Surabaya, which is considered an important and symbolic event in Indonesia's struggle for independence.
