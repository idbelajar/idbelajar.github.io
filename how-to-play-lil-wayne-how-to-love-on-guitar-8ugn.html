Pelajari Settingan Gitar

Kamu harus mengenal gitar kamu terlebih dahulu. Ini akan membantu Anda bermain lebih baik. Kamu bisa memulai dengan belajar bagian-bagian dari gitar, aksesoris tambahan dan setingan gitar. Kenali dengan baik dan pelajari tentang frets dan string, pada khususnya. Pelajari juga mengenai signifikansi mereka dan bagaimana mereka menghasilkan suara dan nada. Pelajari juga mengenai apa itu skala dan akord. Hal yang sama berlaku untuk teknik yang berbeda untuk harmonisasi dan memetik.

Menghafal Kunci dan Teori Gitar (tablature)

Kenali dan pelajari semua kunci mayor dan minor. Pelajari semua kunci dasar seperti C, D, E, F, A, G, beserta semua bentuk b kecil (Bb,Ab) dan #nya . Pelajari juga kunci drop D, drop E dan lainnya. Kunci ini sering dipakai untuk memainkan music metal atau rock. Kamu bisa melihat di buku atau internet.

13. how to play a cassette player


Step1

Check that the cassette player has a power supply. If the player requires batteries, properly install the appropriate batteries into the player according to the diagram printed on the inside of the battery compartment. If the player has a power cable, make sure it is safely plugged into the wall. Connect any speakers, headphones or any other amplifying equipment using the proper cables and jacks. If there is a power switch on the player, turn it to the "On" position

Step2

Find the "Eject" button and press it. Before you put a cassette in the machine, use a pencil or your small finger to turn the sprockets to make sure the magnetic tape inside is taut. If the player is a model with a tape door, the door will open. Place the cassette, tape side fist, in the slot. Gently close the tape door and make sure it is completely shut. If the player requires you to slide the tape in (a car stereo, for example), press "Eject" to make sure the player is empty first, then orient the tape according to the diagram on the tape door and gently push it in.

Step3

Adjust the volume to a low level and press "Play", then adjust the volume accordingly. To stop the tape, press the "Stop" button. To temporarily pause the tape, press the "Pause" button, but note that the player is still in operation in pause mode. To move the tape along to later point, press "Stop" and then "Fast Forward". To remind the tape, press "Stop" then "Rewind".

Step4

Once the tape has reached the end of a side, if your player does not start playing the other side automatically, press "Stop" if need be, then "Eject." Remove the tape, flip it over and re-insert it into the player and press "Play."

Step5

Once you are done using the tape player, either press "Stop" on the player or press "Stop" and turn off the power switch.

15. How to play bentengan step by step


Dalam bahasa INDONESIA :

Berikut adalah tahapan bermain benteng-bentengan :

1. Hompimpa dulu dengan seluruh pemain dan yang kalah maka dia yang jaga.
2. Jika jumlah pemain ada 5 orang, maka jumlah benteng-nya adalah 4.
3. Yang jaga dia tidak mendapat benteng.
4. Setiap pemain harus bertukar benteng dengan pemain lain, namun jangan sampai benteng-nya direbut oleh penjaga.
5. Pemain yang paling banyak gilirannya menjadi penjaga, maka dialah yang kalah.

In ENGLISH :

The following are the stages of playing fortresses ( bentengan ) with:

1. Compile first with all the players and the losers, so they take care.
2. If the number of players is 5 people, then the number of the fort ( benteng ) is 4.
3. The guard ( penjaga ) does not get the fort.
4. Each player must exchange fortifications with other players, but do not let the fort be captured by the guards.
5. The player who has the most turn becomes a guard, so he loses.
