6. The book is on the table. the interrogative form (?) of this sentence is...a. does The book is on the table?b. is The book is on the table?c. are The book is on the table?d. do The book is on the table?


Jawaban:

Bentuk interrogative dari "the book is on the table" adalah :

Is the book on the table?

Jawabannya : B

Penjelasan:

Karena kalimat positive nya bentuk present nominal, dimana rumus nya :

(+) S + To be (am/are/is) + Complement

(-) S + To be (am/are/is) + not + complement

(?) To be (am/are/is) + S + complement

Dan struktur dari kalimat "The book is on the table" :

The book => Subject

Is => To be

On the table => Adverb of place

9. the sum of four number is 211 the average of three of the number is 42. what is the four number ?


Pelajaran:Matematika

Arti pertanyaan tersebut dalam Bahasa Indonesia:

Jumlah dari 4 bilangan adalah 211 dan rata-rata 3 bilangan adalah 42.Berapakah nilai dari bilangan ke-4 tersebut.

Jawab:

Misal:

Bilangan 1=A

Bilangan 2=B

Bilangan 3=C

Bilangan 4=D

A+B+C+D=211

42=A+B+C÷3

A+B+C=42×3

A+B+C=126

Nilai bilangan keempat(D)=211-126=85.
