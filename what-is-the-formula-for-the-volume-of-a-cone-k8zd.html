15. ##Quiz #75 ##• english version• big point• very easy• no spam==========a cone lies inside a cylinder with the same diameter 12cm. the volume of the cone 345 cm³. if the height of the cone is one third of the cylinder, find the volume of the cylinder. (π= 3,14)​


Answer

-

[tex]volume \: cone = \frac{1}{3} \times \pi \times {r}^{2} \times t[/tex]

[tex]345 = \frac{1}{3} \times 3,14 \times {6}^{2} \times t[/tex]

[tex]345 = \frac{1}{3} \times 3,14 \times 6 \times 6 \times t[/tex]

[tex]345 = 3,14 \times 2 \times 6 \times t[/tex]

[tex]345 = 37,68t[/tex]

[tex]t = \frac{345}{37,68} [/tex]

[tex]t = 9,15605096 \: cm[/tex]

_____________________________________

[tex]height \: cone = \frac{1}{3} \: height \: cylinder[/tex]

[tex]9,15605096 = \frac{1}{3 } \times t \: cylinder[/tex]

[tex]t \: cylinder = 27,4681529 \: cm[/tex]

_____________________________________

[tex]volume \: cylinder = \pi \times {r}^{2} \times t[/tex]

[tex]volume \: cylinder = 3,14 \times {6}^{2} \times 27,4681529[/tex]

[tex]volume \: cylinder = 3,14 \times 6 \times 6 \times 27,4681529[/tex]

[tex]volume \: cylinder = 3.105 \: c {m}^{3} [/tex]

_____________________________________

volume of the cylinder is 3.105 cm³

Jawab:

3.104,52 cm³

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Given that:

A cone is located inside a cylinder

base diameter (d) = 12 cmVcone = 345 cm³hcone = 1/3(hcylinder)Value of π ≈ 3,14

Find the volume of the cylinder (Vcylinder)

The equation for Vcone = (π×d²×h)/12

Find the height of the cone (hcone)

345 ≈ (3,14×12²×h)/12

345 ≈ (452,16×h)/12

452,16h ≈ 345×12

452,16h ≈ 4140

h ≈ 4140/452,16

hcone ≈ 9,15 cm

hcone = 1/3(hcylinder)

therefore

9,15 ≈ 1/3(hcylinder)

hcylinder ≈ 9,15 / (1/3)

hcylinder ≈ 9,15 × 3

hcylinder ≈ 27,45 cm

The equation for Vcylinder = (π×d²×h)/4

Therefore

Vcylinder ≈ (π×12²×27,45)/4 ≈

3.104,52 cm³ ✅

The volume of the cylinder located outside the cone is approximately 3.104,52 cubic centimetres, if not being rounded. ✅
