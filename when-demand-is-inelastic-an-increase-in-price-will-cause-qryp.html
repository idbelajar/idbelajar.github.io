Pembahasan

10. Why does increase in price cause an increase in supply? (Mengapa kenaikan harga menyebabkan naiknya penawaran?)

Kata kuncinya adalah "increase, in, price, dan supply". Perhatikan kalimat ke-9 dan ke-10!

An increase in the price of goods causes an increase in supply the number of good that producers make. Producers will make better when they can have higher pieces for the goods.

Dengan kenaikan harga barang, produsen akan membuat barang yang lebih bagus pula. Jadi, jawaban yang paling tepat adalah "because producers make better goods when prices are high".

Jawaban A.

11. why does a decrese in prices cause an increase in demand? (Mengapa penurunan harga menyebabkan naiknya permintaan?)

Kata kuncinya adalah "decrease, in, price, demand". Perhatikan kalimat ke-11, 12, and 13!

At the same time an increase in the prices of the goods causes a decrease in demand-the number of good the consumers buy. This is because people buy less when the price high. Conversely, a decrease in the price causes an increase in demand and a decrease in supply.

Orang-orang membeli lebih sedikit saat harga naik. Sebaliknya, orang-orang membeli lebih banyak saat harga turun. Jadi, jawaban yang paling tepat adalah "consumers (people) buy more goods when prices are low".

Jawaban D.

12. What do a business firms look at when they make decisions about prices and production? (Apa yang perusahaan lihat ketika mereka membuat keputusan tentang harga dan produksi?)

Kata kuncinya adalah "business, firms, look, make, decision". Perhatikan kalimat ke-14 dan 15!

Business firms look at both supply and demand when they make decisions about prices and production. They look for the equilibrium point where supply equals demand.

Perusahaan membuat keputusan dengan melihat titik kesetimbangan antara permintaan dan penawaran. Jadi, jawaban yang paling tepat adalah "They look at equilibrium point".

Jawaban B.

13. Why will the consumers and producers agree about the equilibrium price? (Mengapa konsumen dan produsen akan setuju dengan harga keseimbangan?)

Kata kuncinya adalah "consumers, producers, agree, aquilibrium". Perhatikan kalimat ke-22 dan 23!

According to the Law of Supply and Demand, the equilibrium price is the best price for the goods. The consumers and the producers will agree on this price because it is only price that help them both equally.

Harga keseimbangan akan membatu kedua pihak dengan sama (adil). Jadi, jawaban yang paling tepat adalah "it will help them both equally".

Jawaban D.

14. When will the producers have a surplus of goods? (Kapan produsen akan memiliki kelebihan barang?)

Kata kuncinya adalah "producers, surplus". Perhatikan kalimat ke-19 dan 20!

If the producers increase the price or if they produce more, the consumers will not buy all of those goods. The producers will have a surplus more supply than demand-so they must decrease the price in order to sell all of the goods.

Kelebihan barang terjadi karena harga barang naik, permintaan menurun. Jadi, jawaban yang paling tepat adalah "It will happen when there is more supply than demand".

Jawaban B.
