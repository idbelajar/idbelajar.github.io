7. Need is the mother of invention. Do you think this saying is applicable to the development of technical writing? Discuss how it is applicable and support your discussion with reasob


Need is the mother of invention. Ungkapan ini lebih kurang berarti "Kebutuhan adalah pangkal inovasi" atau "Kebutuhan adalah sumber lahirnya inovasi". Pada dasarnya, ungkapan ini benar adanya. Hal ini dikarenakan sebagai manusia, hidup kita senantiasa bergantung pada hukum penawaran dan permintaan. Penawaran ada karena permintaan. Demikian juga halnya dengan inovasi/penemuan. Penemuan lahir karena adanya masalah/atau kebutuhan.

Untuk mendukung kebenaran ungkapan ini, kamu dapat menggunakan pernyataan berikut:
Need is the mother of invention. This formula applies not only to science but also technical writing. In technical writing, we draft contents to make the target readers understand the subject easily. For example, we use technical writing for a PC application support, such as Windows, to let the users know every detail about using Windows. That includes troubleshooting, what Windows is, error logs, and everything else. As our needs grow more complex, we also need a better solution from the past. The functions get more complicated and not everyone can learn and understand how it works. This is where the need for technical writing comes. People need to understand how the new technology works, especially how it can help with their problems. To solve this problem, we use technical writing to create a user-friendly draft about the new technology, such as FAQ, user-friendly knowledge base, support center, various documentation etc. 

Basically, this is how technical writing correlates with our needs. The more our needs grow, the more we need technical writing.

Simpulan:
Our needs create demands for a better and easier to understand technical writing. It grows based on our needs.
