4. 25 gram of alloy composed of copper(Cu) and zinc (Zn) is reacted with HCl, and 4,48 L of hydrogen is obtained at STP. What is the mass presentage of copper in the alloy? catatan : copper tidak beraksi dengan HCl. mohon tuliskan caranya


Zn + 2 HCl => ZnCl2 + H2
V H2 STP = 4,48 liter
V STP = 22,4 liter/mol
mol H2 = 4,48 liter / 22,4 liter/mol = 0,2 mol
mol Zn = mol H2 (koefisien sama) = 0,2 mol
massa Zn = 0,2 mol x 65 gr/mol = 13 gram
massa Cu = 25 - 13 = 12 gram
% Cu = 12 gr/25gr  x 100% = 48%
