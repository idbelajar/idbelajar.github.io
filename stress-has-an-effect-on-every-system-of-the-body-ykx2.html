8. Even at low levels,_. A.The nervous system has produced detrimental effects by lead B. Lead's detrimental effects are producing the nervous systen C. Lead produces detrimental effect on the nervous system D. The detrimental effects produced by lead on the nervous system


Jawaban:

Even at low levels,_.

C. Lead produces detrimental effect on the nervous system.

Penjelasan:

Perhatikan opsi jawaban.

A.The nervous system has produced detrimental effects by lead.

Kalimat pasif. Kurang kata 'been".

Seharusnya: The nervous system has been produced detrimental effects by lead.

B. Lead's detrimental effects are producing the nervous system.

Tense dari kalimat ini salah, karena kalimat tersebut adalah kalimat fakta. Seharusnya menggunakan simple present tenes:

Seharusnya:Lead's detrimental effects produces the nervous system.

C. Lead produces detrimental effect on the nervous system.

Kalimat ini menggunakan simple present tense dengan subject uncountable noun (lead).

D. The detrimental effects produced by lead on the nervous system.

Kalimat pasif. Kurang auxiliary verb.

Seharusnya: D. The detrimental effects are produced by lead on the nervous system.
