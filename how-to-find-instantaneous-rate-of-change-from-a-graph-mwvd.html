Distance climbs=distance down

v1×t1=v2×t2

1.5t1=4.5t2

t1/t2=4.5/1.5

=3/1

Since he gone for 6 hours its mean he climbs and down for 6 hr

So we have t1=3/4×6 hour=4.5 hour

Distance to climbs=v1×t1=1.5×4.5=6.75 kilometers
