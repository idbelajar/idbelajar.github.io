4. How to make computer


To build a computer that functions, you only need-

-a Hard Drive
-a Processor with fan and heatsink
-a motherboard
-a Monitor
-256mb or more of ram
-a Power Supply Unit-(PSU)
-a mouse
-a keyboard
-cables to connect it all

But to add form to function you need, in addition to that above-

-a case - AT, ATX, BTX, or LPX -a modem
-an ethernet card
-CD/DVD drives

and if you want it to be even better-

-a graphics card 
-a sound card
-speakers
-another hard drive

10. Buatlah percakapan dalam 5 org dengan menggunakan b.inggris berjudul how to do move whatsapp application to computer


Membuat percakapan bahasa inggris dengan topik “How To Move Whatsapp Application to Computer” adalah dengan membuat 5 karakter yang 4 diantaranya menyimak langkah-langkah dari 1 orang karakater. Berikut adalah contohnya:


Pembahasan
Dialog How to move Whatsapp application to computer

Ellen: Hans, could you help me? My father was asking me how to move the Whatsapp application to his computer, but I don’t know how to do it.

Hans: Wait a moment Ellen, I think Josh knows how to do it. Right?

Josh: Well, yeah I have done that in the past.

Klara: Oh wow you guys are geek. Explain to us maybe one day it will be useful.

Luna: Yeah! I’m listening here…

Josh: Okay, so. Firstly turn on your phone and search whatsapp application.

Ellen: Wait hang on, I’m taking a note.

Hans: And then?

Josh: After that, search barcode in the setting application.

Klara: Oh I know where it’s located.

Josh: Yes. Open and turn on your laptop. And then search in google: Whatsapp application.

Luna: What’s next?

Josh: Next use the barcode of QR scanner from whatsapp to scan the QR code, using the phone camera.

Ellen: Is that all?

Josh: Yes, and finally your Whatsapp is available on the laptop.

ALL: Thanks Josh!!!
