CAUSE AND EFFECT

what is the pattern of cause and effect in sentence

JAWABAN:

There are two sentence structures of cause and effect:

Due to/Because of/owing to/Thanks to + noun Because/Since/As/For/As a result of + subject + verb

PEMBAHASAN:

=> Apa pola sebab dan akibat dalam kalimat?

Ada dua pola kalimat dari cause and effect:

Due to/Because of/owing to/Thanks to + kata benda Because/Since/As/For/As a result of + subjek + kata kerja

Contoh kalimat cause and effect:

Due to his good personality, he was elected as exemplary student. (Karena kepribadiannya yang baik, ia terpilih sebagai siswa teladan)

Cause : his good personality

Effect : he was elected as exemplary student

Signal word : Due to

Because of you, I will stay here until the end of the time. (Karena kamu, saya akan tinggal di sini sampai akhir waktu)

Cause : you

Effect : I will stay here until the end of the time

Signal word : Because of

She became really ashamed owing to the incident in the stage when she forgot the lyrics of the song. (Dia menjadi sangat malu karena insiden di panggung ketika dia lupa lirik lagu tersebut)

Cause : he incident in the stage when she forgot the lyrics of the song

Effect : She became really ashamed

Signal word : owing to  

I can continue my study abroad easily thanks to your guidance for me. (Saya dapat melanjutkan studi saya di luar negeri dengan mudah berkat bimbingan Anda untuk saya)

Cause : your guidance for me

Effect : I can continue my study abroad easily

Signal word : thanks to

Ammar studies hard every day because he wants to be a good doctor someday. (Ammar belajar keras setiap hari karena dia ingin menjadi dokter yang baik suatu hari nanti)

Cause : he wants to be a good doctor someday

Effect : Ammar studies hard every day

Signal word : because

Since Ameera was sick yesterday, she didn't come to school. (Karena Ameera sakit kemarin, dia tidak datang ke sekolah)

Cause : Ameera was sick yesterday

Effect : she didn't come to school

Signal word : since

She got so many gift from her friends as it was her birthday. (Dia mendapat banyak hadiah dari teman-temannya karena itu adalah hari ulang tahunnya)

Cause : it was her birthday

Effect : She got so many gift from her friends

Signal word : as

I felt very boring for I was waiting for my fiend in the air port. (Saya merasa sangat bosan karena saya sedang menunggu teman saya di bandara)

Cause : I was waiting for my fiend in the air port

Effect : I felt very boring

Signal word : for

I could not go to Bandung this morning as a result of I was late . (Saya tidak bisa pergi ke Bandung pagi ini karena saya terlambat)

Cause : I was late

Effect : I could not go to Bandung this morning

Signal word : as a result of
