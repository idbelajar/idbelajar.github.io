14. Type a bacteria multiply every five minutes. in the first fifteenminutes there were 400 bacteria. the number of bacteria in thefirst thirty-five minutes was ...​


Jawaban:

3200 bakteri

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Pembahasan

Barisan Geometri

Rumus

Un = a.r^n-1

Penjelasan

diketahui :

U_4 = 400

r = 2

ditanya :

U_7

jawab:

cari a terlebih dahulu

U_4 = a.r^n-1

400 = a. 2^4-1

400 = a. 2^3

400 = a . 8

a = 50

selanjutnya kita cari U_7

U_7 = a. r^n-1

U_7 = 50. 2^7-1

U_7 = 50. 2^6

U_7 = 50. 64

U_7 = 3200

Jadi banyak bakteri 35 menit pertama adalah 3200
