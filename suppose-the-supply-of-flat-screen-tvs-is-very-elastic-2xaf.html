11. The perimeter of a rectangular TV screen is 150 cm. The ratio of the width to the height is 16:9. Find the area of the screen.​


Answer/Jawaban:

1296 cm^2

Explanation :

Given the perimeter is 150 cm, meanwhile the ratio of the width and the height is 16 : 9. Assume the width and the height is 16x and 9x. We can substitute to the perimeter formula :

P = 2(16x + 9x) = 150 cm

<=> 2.25x = 150 cm

<=> 50x = 150 cm

<=> x = 3 cm

So, the width is 16 x 3 cm = 48 cm, meanwhile the height is 9 x 3 cm = 27 cm.

We can conclude that the area of the screen is 48 x 27 = 1296 cm^2.

Penjelasan :

Diketahui kelilingnya 150 cm, sedangkan perbandingan lebar dan tingginya 16 : 9. Misalkan lebar dan tingginya adalah 16x dan 9x. Kita bisa masukkan ke rumus keliling :

P = 2(16x + 9x) = 150 cm

<=> 2.25x = 150 cm

<=> 50x = 150 cm

<=> x = 3 cm

Jadi, lebarnya adalah 16 x 3 cm = 48 cm, sedangkan tingginya adalah 9 x 3 cm = 27 cm.

Kita bisa simpulkan bahwa luas layarnya adalah 48 x 27 = 1296 cm^2.


12. Explain and give examples: 1. Perishable goods 2. Not perishable goods 3. Low of supply 4. Glut on the market 5. Elastic supply


1. Goods that must be used in a short period of time; goods that are able to perish, decay, or spoil rapidly.
ex: fresh meat, ripe fruits, seafood, etc.

2. Goods that have long shelf life and commonly don't require refrigeration.
ex: sugar, flour, rice, candies. etc.

3. Running out of supply; a supply shortage. 
ex: I am low on milk. I have to buy some soon.

4. Something that is on the market in a great abundance; a flood of something in the market; something that fill beyond the capacity of the market.
ex: It is the mango season. The mangoes are a glut on the market.

5. Supply of a good or service that increases or decreases as the price of an item goes up or down.
ex: The price of the stones has been high on the market nowadays. Therefore, there is an increase on the quantity of the stones supply.

Ps.

Depiction of a producer behavior at the time of changes in the prices of goods or services.
