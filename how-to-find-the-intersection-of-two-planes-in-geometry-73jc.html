2. Quiz (571/580)Geometry :A circle has a circumference of 44 cm, find the area of ​​the circle = ...​


Hint:

circumference of circle = 44 cm.

Problematic:

What is the area of circle = ..?

Step by step explanation:

Find the radius of that circle:

Cf = 22/7 × 2r

44 cm = 22/7 × 2r

2r = 44 cm ÷ 22/7

2r = 14 cm

r = 14 cm : 2

r = 7 cm

Find the area of that circle:

A = 22/7 × r²

A = 22/7 × (7 cm)²

A = 22/7 × 49 cm²

A = 22 × 7 cm²

A = 154 cm²

So, the area of that circle is 154 cm²
