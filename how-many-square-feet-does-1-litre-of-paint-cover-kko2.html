2. a truck travels 0.72 km on 0.01 litre of fuel how many kilomatetres will it travel on 1 litre of fuel?


Soal ini dapat dikerjakan dengan cara perbandingan senilai

0,72 km → 0,01 L

x km → 1 L

.

[tex] \frac{0,72}{x} [/tex] = [tex] \frac{0,01}{1} [/tex]

0,72 = x . 0,01

[tex] \frac{0,72}{0,01} [/tex] = x

72 = x

Maka, jarak yg dapat ditempuh dengan 1 L bensin adalah 72 km

20 litre = 20.000 ml paint needed.

Ingredients

200 ml paint = 40g paint powder and 120 ml water.

If one portion of ingredients equals to 200 ml paint, then the total portion needed:

20.000 ml / 200 ml = 100 portions

The total of paint powder needed

100 × 40g = 4000g = 4kg paint powder

Packet of paint powder

= 4 kg / 0.6 kg

= 6,66 packet

=> 7 packets needed (rounded up)
