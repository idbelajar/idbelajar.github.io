4. Two cars traveled 450km. the speed of the second car every hour is 15 km faster than the speed of the first car. if the second car travel time is 1 hour shorter than the first car travel time. then the average speed of the two cars is .....


Pembahasan

<<Diketahui>>

jarak = 450 km.

kecepatan = 15 km lebih cepat dari kecepatan mobil pertama

<<Ditanya>>

kecepatan rata-rata kedua mobil = ....?

<<Penyelesaian>>

[tex]\boxed{Va\times t=Vb(t-1)}\\Va\times t=(Va+15)(t-1)\\=Va\times t-Va+15t-15\\Va=15t-15\\Va\times t=450\\Va=\frac{450}{t}[/tex]

Selanjutnya Kita akan Substitusikan ke Va=15t-15

[tex]15t-15=\frac{450}{t} \\15t^{2} -15=0\rightarrow menbagi~kedua~ruas~dengan~15\\t^{2} -t-30=0\\(t-6)(t+5)=0\\~~~~~~~~~~~~~~t=6~Jam\\Maka.\\Va=\frac{450}{6}\\Va=75\\Vb=75+15\\Vb=90\\Jadi,rata-rata~kecepatan~kedua~mobil\\=\frac{90+75 }{2} \\=\frac{165}{2} \\=82,5\frac{km}{jam}[/tex]
