3. How many grams are in 1.2 x 10²⁴ molecules of water?​


Answer

Mass of water = 36 grams

Explanation

Molecules of water is written in formula : H₂O,

to calculate the mass of the water, we should know about realtive molecular mass of water from periodic table :

Mr H₂O = 2 x Ar H + Ar O = 2 x 1 + 16 = 18 g/mole

From Avogadro number ==>

[tex]\boxed{mole\ of\ water\ = \frac{Molecules\ number\ of \ Water}{6,02\ .\ 10^{23} } }[/tex]

mole of water = 1.2 x 10²⁴ /(6.02 x 10²³) = 1.99 mole = 2 mole

Mass of water = mole of water x Mr water = 2 mole x 18 g/mole

= 36 grams of water

13. A pot contained twice as much water as a bottle. The total amount of water in both the pot and the bottle was 5 l. What was the amount of the water in the bottle?​


Jawaban:

5/3 liters of water

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

pot = 2 × bottle

pot + bottle = 5 liters

substitute pot with x and bottle with y.

x = 2y

x + y = 5

substitute x with 2y into the equation.

x + y = 5

2y + y = 5

3y = 5

y = 5/3 liters

the amount of water inside the bottle was 5/3 liters.

x = 2y

x = 2(5/3)

x = 10/3

the amount of water inside the pot was 10/3 liters.
